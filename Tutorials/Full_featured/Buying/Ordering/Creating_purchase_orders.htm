eSuite Buy - How to Create Purchase Orders

eSuite Buy > Buy > Order

Information

The eSuite Buy module offers the tools for creating and editing purchase orders.

This document shows how to:

  • Create and edit a purchase order.
  • Add items.
  • Remove items.
  • Manage quantities.
  • Delete a purchase order
  • Suspend or close the purchase order.

Creating a purchase order

Purchase orders may be created using the New option in the start menu or from the list of vendors.

To create a purchase order from the New option:

  1. In the launcher, click the arrow in the Buy entry to display its menu.
  2. Click on Order: the Start window opens on the desktop.

     
  3. Click on New: the New document window opens on the desktop.

  4. Select a vendor from the Vendor field.
  5. Click on Create to confirm the creation of a new purchase order for the selected vendor.

To create a purchase order from the list of vendors:

  1. In the start menu, click on Vendors or Vendors - All.
  2. Select a vendor from the list and click on Action in the bottom menu.
  3. Click on New document.

When a purchase order is created:

  • Its status is OPEN.
  • The Full Document option in the start menu is enabled.
    This gives access to the Document window with information on the purchase order and tools to edit it.
  • The new purchase order is listed in the Open document grid that is available from the Documents and Documents - All options in the start menu.

Adding items to the purchase order

The second step in the creation of a purchase order is adding items.

  1. Select Items in the Start window to open the item search wizard.
    The Vendors from current documents checkbox is selected by default and disabled: only items from the current vendor will be displayed in the resulting grid.

     
  2. Add all necessary criteria and click Execute: the list of items opens on the desktop.
    The Case, Unit, Weight and Cost fields in the Order column are editable depending on the UoM.

     
  3. Double-click in any editable field and add the quantities to order.
    Press Enter after each new value.

     
  4. Click on Save in the toolbar: the new values are displayed in the Order and Total columns.

     

Editing the purchase order

Before closing the purchase order, you may want to revise and edit the information in it. This is done through the Document window.

  1. From the start menu, click on Documents - All: the list of purchase orders opens in the Open document grid.
  2. Select a purchase order.
  3. Right-click to display the list of options.

     
  4. Click on Open.
  5. Click on Full document

Editing general information

General information related to vendor and shipping may be edited in the Transaction header table.

  1. From the Document window, click on Edit header in the upper part of the document.
    The Transaction header table opens on the desktop.

      
  2. Make all necessary changes.
  3. Click on Apply changes in the toolbar.

Editing specific information

Information related to items may be edited in the Transaction line order table.

  1. From the Document window, select an item in the Lines section.
  2. Click on Edit selected line in the upper part of the section.
    The Transaction line order table opens on the desktop.

  3. Make all the necessary changes.
  4. Click on Apply changes in the toolbar.

For details on the fields and functionalities, see Transaction line order table.

Editing lines

Quantities (cases, units, weight) and amounts of the purchase order may be modified directly in the Lines section.

  1. Double-click in the case, unit or weight column of the item.
  2. Enter or select the new value.
  3. If necessary, modify the amount.

  4. Click Save all changes in the toolbar of the Lines section.

Voiding items

Items may be removed from the purchase order.

  1. In the Lines section of the Document window, select the item to remove.
  2. Right-click to display the menu.
  3. Click on Void selected line: a confirmation message is prompted.

  4. Click on Yes to delete the item from the purchase order.

Deleting a purchase order

Open purchase orders may be deleted from the Document window:

  1. In the toolbar, click on Cancel: a confirmation mesage is prompted:

  2. Click on Yes to delete the order.
  3. In the Open document grid, click on Refresh in the bottom menu: the deleted purchase order is not listed any more.

Suspending a purchase order

Open purchase orders may be suspended and edited at a later time.

  1. Open the Document window for the purchase order.
  2. Click on Suspend the document in the toolbar.
    The Document window closes.

To open a suspended purchase order:

  1. From the Open documents grid, right-click on the suspended purchase order.
  2. Select Open from the menu.
    The purchase order is available from the Full Document  option in the start menu.

Closing the purchase order

Purchase orders need to be closed for the receiving to be intiated.

  1. From the list of documents, open the purchase order.
  2. From the Start window, click on Full Document: the Document window opens on the desktop.
  3. Click on Close document in the window toolbar: a confirmation message is prompted.

  4. Click on Yes to close the order.
  5. In the Open document grid, click on Refresh in the bottom menu: the closed purchase order is not listed any more.

When a purchase order is closed, it is automatically transferred to the list of open documents in the receiving.

NOTE: It is not possible to reopen a closed purchase order.
