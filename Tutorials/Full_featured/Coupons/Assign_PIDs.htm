eSuite - How to Assign Promotion IDs to Items

eSuite > Items > Master > Item list

Information

Promotion IDs, often used as coupon criteria, group items that are part of the same promotions.

The eSuite software provides an efficient way of assigning promotions.

Assigning promotion IDs to items

  1. In the Items - General list, select the items that will be part of the same promotion:
  2. Click Actions in the bottom menu:
  3. Select Assign promotion ID. In the Action window, enter the promotion ID to assign to the items and press OK:
  4. Press OK to confirm then click on Yes:
  5. From the Items entry in the Launcher, select Auxiliary tables then Promotion IDs:
  6.  In the Promotion ID grid, select the newly-created promotion.
    Press Items in the Action bottom menu:
  7. This displays the list of items that are part of the selected promotion:

Back to top

Unassigning promotion IDs from items

  1. In the Items - General list, select the items the promotion ID will be unassigned from:
  2. Click Actions in the bottom menu and select Unassign promotion ID.
  3. In the Action window, enter the promotion ID to unassign from the selected item(s) and press OK:
  4. Press OK to confirm then click Yes:
