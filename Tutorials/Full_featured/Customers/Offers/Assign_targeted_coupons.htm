eSuite Care - Assigning Targeted Coupons to Customers

eSuite Care > Offers > Offer Items

Information

Targeted coupons are store and vendor coupons given by the system when the specified criteria are reached. They are created with the coupon wizard utility and assigned to a single customer or to multiple customers using the Link offer table.

Targeted coupons require the Bank_SMSPayment option.

Assigning targeted coupons to a single customer

This is how to create a targeted coupon for a free bag of Oreo cookies and assign it to customer 2, Mary Petersen. The customer has to clip the offer from the store website.

1. Create the targeted coupon with the coupon wizard utility.
 
2. From the Offers entry in the launcher, select Targeted offers:
 
3. Enter the targeted coupon information in the wizard and click Execute: the Offers - Items list displays the coupon corresponding to the search criteria.
4. Select the targeted coupon and click Assign offer in the Action bottom menu:
 
 
5. The Link offer table opens, displaying the targeted coupon information:
 
 
6. Add the following information:
  Customer code field: enter 2 for Mary Petersen's customer code or click on the arrow to select the code 2 from the Customer code list.
  Offer quantity field: enter 3.
This indicates that the customer can get this offer three times.
  Check the Clip needed box (optional).
This indicates that the customer has to clip the offer from the store website.
  Add a description to the Long description field (optional). 
 
 
7. Click Save in the toolbar to complete the offer.

To display the offer details:

  • Select the targeted coupon in the Offers - Items list.
  • Click Analyze, then Offer details in the bottom menu of the list.
  • The offer details open, displaying information related to the customer, the offer and the movement:

Assigning targeted coupons to multiple customers

This is how to create a targeted coupon for $0.50 off on two (2) cream corn items and assign it to a group of customers that includes customer 2, Mary Petersen.

 1. Create the targeted coupon with the coupon wizard utility. 
 
  
2. From the Offers entry in the launcher, select the Targeted offers option:
 
 
3. Enter the targeted coupon information in the wizard and click Execute: the Offers - Items list displays the coupon corresponding to the search criteria.
4. In the lower left corner of the launcher, click Add to current desktop.
   
 
5. From the Customers menu in the launcher, open the Customers - General list. The list of offers and the list of customers are both open on the same desktop. 
   
6. In Offers - Items, select the targeted coupon. In Customers - General list, select the customers to assign the targeted coupon to.
 
 
7. Click Action in the bottom menu of the customer list and select Assign offer (F4).
 
 
8. The Link offer table opens, displaying the targeted coupon information.
    The Customer code field displays the code of the record that was last selected in the customer list.
 
 
9. Add the following information:
  Offer quantity field: enter 1.
  Add a description to the Long description field (optional). 
 
10. Click Global to assign the targeted offer to the selected customers. A message requesting to confirm the action is displayed:
 
 
11. Click Yes: a message warning that the offer has been assigned to the three customers is displayed.
 
 
12. Click OK.
13. Click Save in the bottom of the Link offer window to complete the offer.

To display the offer details:

  • Select the targeted coupon in the Offers - Items list.
  • Click Analyze, then Offer details in the bottom menu of the list.
  • The offer details open, displaying information related to the customers, the offer and the movement:
