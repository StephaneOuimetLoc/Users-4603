Basic Reports

eSuite General

Information

Dimensions reports give an overview of the selected dimension (department, sub-department, category, etc.) in terms of sales, profit movement, etc.

To access the dimensions reports:

  • In the launcher, click on Dimensions.
  • Click on the right entry among Financials, Department, Sub-departments, Categories, Families, Report codes, Vendors, Hourly: the Start window opens, displaying the dimension-related options.

The following dimension-related reports are available:

  • Single total
  • Multi totals
  • Compare totals

Single total

The Single total wizard offers criteria selection that may include period, totalizer, target, dimension range. Depending on the dimension, it may allow breaking down information by date, by store and by item.

 

The Single total report displays one sum for all the selected totalizers together.

In the bottom menu:

  • Edit provides access to the selected dimension table and allows editing (F3).
    It may give access to images.
  • Analyze allows breaking down information by date, by store or by item and performing subqueries and linked queries.
    It provides access to further reports.

Multi totals

The Multi totals wizard offers criteria selection that may include period, target, totalizer range, dimension range. Depending on the dimension, it may allow breaking down information by date, by store and by item and showing only the dimensions with no movement. Criteria related to movement may be added with the Add criteria button.

The Multi totals report displays details for all the selected totalizers.

In the bottom menu:

  • Edit provides access to the selected dimension table and allows editing (F3).
    It may give access to images.
  • Analyze allows breaking down information by date, by store or by item and performing subqueries and linked queries.
    It provides access to further reports.

Compare totals

The Compare totals wizard offers criteria selection that may include period, target, target compare, totalizer, totalizer compare, dimension range. Depending on the dimension, it may allow breaking down information by date, by store and by item and showing only the dimensions with no movement. Criteria related to movement may be added with the Add criteria button.

The Compare totals report displays transaction quantities and total sales from a store comparing to another and/or a totalizer compare to another by dimension.

In the bottom menu:

  • Edit: provides access to the selected dimension table and allows editing (F3).
    It may give access to images.
  • Analyze: allows breaking down information by date, by store or by item and performing subqueries and linked queries.
    It provides access to further reports.
