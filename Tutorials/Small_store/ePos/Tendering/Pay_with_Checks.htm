Table of contents

Information

The check tender is used when customers pay using checks. The checks must be accepted by the system for the customers. (see shopper level and risk level information). The system can be programmed for the check verification. See your reseller for more details.

To use the check tender, please follow the next steps:-

  1. Log into ePos
  2. Enter customer ID number
  3. Scan/enter items
  4. go to Functions
  5. Go to Tenders
  6. Select Check
  7. A message "Check?" will be displayed in the entry window
  8. Enter an amount
  9. A bank entry form will pop up for you to fill in the customer's bank information
  10. A validation window will pop up for you to select the validation type you would like displayed.
  11. The amount entered will be displayed on the POS screen. Complete your transaction.
