eSuite - Mix & Match Table

eSuite > Items > Item auxialaries > Mix & Matches

Information

The Mix & Match table contains mix & match entries used to mix items to match them to a price.

NOTE: The mix & match records do not contain or keep track of quantities, amounts, discounts or mix & match types. They are only used to link items to mix them and then match them to the price defined in the Price table.

Displaying the Mix & Match table

In the Launcher, click the arrow in the Items entry to display its menu and then:

  1. Click on Auxiliary tables: the Start window opens in the Auxiliary work area.
  2. Click on Mix & matches: the Mixes tab opens, displaying the Mix & Match list.
  3. To modify an existing mix & match record, select it in the list and double-click on it, press the F3 key on the keyboard or click the Edit command in the bottom menu: the Mix & Match table opens, overlapping the work area.
  4. To create a mix & match record, click on Add in the bottom menu of the grid: the Mix & Match table opens, overlapping the work area.

Content

Here is a description of the Mix & Match table fields and their functionalities:

FIELD DESCRIPTION
Number The Number field contains the mix & match number.
It is a required field used to uniquely identify records in the Mix & Match list.
Descriptor The Descriptor field contains the description of the mix & match entry.
It is not a required field, however a descriptor should entered to identify the mix & match record in the Mix & Match list.
