Accosssunting Overview                       

eSuite Manage > Accounting

Information

The Accounting entry records and processes accounting transactions within modules such as general ledger, accounts receivable, accounts payable and labor.

The eSuite software keeps the accounting information in different grids and tables that are accessible from the Accounting menu in the launcher:

The accounting entry is split into several sub-entries:

  • Ledger
    • G/L posting
    • Export list
    • G/L totalizer setup
  • Receivable
    • Accounts summary
    • Age of accounts
    • Account interest
    • Reports
  • Payable
    • A/P posting
    • Export list
    • A/P totalizer setup
  • Items
  • Labor
