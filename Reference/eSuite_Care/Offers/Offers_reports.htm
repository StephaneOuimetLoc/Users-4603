eSuite Care - Offers Reports

eSuite Care > Offers

Information

The Offers reports allow analysis and follow-up of store and vendor discounts. They provide information such as the discount value, the type of offers assigned and used by customers, etc.

The Offers reports are available from eSuite Care:

  1. In the launcher, click on Offers.
  2. Select the right type of report from the Start window.

Offers reports:

  • Targeted offers
  • Details
  • Assigned offers
  • Details
  • Redemptions
  • Offer tasks

Targeted offers

The Targeted offers wizard offers criteria selection that includes offer code and description, sub-department, category, family and report code. It displays the unassigned offers only. The More criteria button adds further offer-related criteria.

The Offers - Items list displays the electronic offers available to customers.

In the bottom menu:

  • Edit opens the Coupon table for the modification of the selected offer and the creation of new offers.
  • Analyze displays the offer details.
    It provides access to queries for further-drill down to previous results (offers, items and customers).
  • Action allows assigning the offer, creting GS1 coupons (currently under development) and creating offers.

Assigned offers

The Assigned offers wizard offers criteria selection that includes offer code and description, customer, category, family and report code. It allows breaking down the report information by customer.

The Assigned offers report displays the list of targeted offers assigned to customers, including clip or ready to use offer information, the exposure total value and the used total value by offer.

In the bottom menu:

  • Edit opens the Coupon table for the modification of the selected offer and the creation of new offers.
  • Analyze allows to display the offer details.
    It offers tools for performing subqueries and linked queries to further drill down to previous results (offers, items and customers).
  • Action allows assigning the offer, creating GS1 coupons (currently under development) and creating offers.

Details

The Offers - details wizard provides criteria selection that includes target. The More criteria button adds criteria related to offer values, date, account, marketing and others.

The Offers - details report displays the targeted offers movement by the customer. It includes information on the customer, value, the quantity assigned, used, left, and the exposure.

In the bottom menu:

  • Edit opens the Coupon table for the modification of the selected offer and the creation of new offers.
  • Analyze displays the offer details.
    It provides access to subqueries and linked queries for further drill-down to previous results (offers, items and customers).
  • Action allows assigning the offer, creating GS1 coupons (currently under development) and creating offers.

Redemptions

The Offers - redemptions wizard provides criteria selection that includes target and allows breaking down the report information by date, by store, by totalizer, by coupon, by item and by customer. Criteria related to account, offer, item and totalizer may be added with the Add criteria button.

The Offers - redemptions report displays the total discount of the targeted offers used and total sales information. The More criteria button adds redemption-related criteria on customer account IDs, offer code and type, item codes and totalizers.

In the bottom menu:

  • Analyze allows breaking the report information by transaction.
    It provides access to subqueries and linked queries for further drill-down to previous results (customers, items and coupons).
    NOTE: To use subqueries and linked queries, break down the report information by customer, by item or by coupon in the Offers - redemptions wizard.
