eSuite - How to Add a Delivery Schedule

eSuite > Dimensions

Information

Delivery schedules give details on the deliveries scheduled for a vendor. This information is available in the delivery section of the Vendor table. A vendor can have an unlimited number of delivery schedules.

The date and time for the orders and the receiving can be programmed. These schedules can be used for different utilities:

  • Creating orders automatically through a schedule
  • Suggesting the right date for the orders and the deliveries when the orders are created manually
  • Closing the orders automatically when they are due to be sent to the vendor
  • Figuring out the cost and the price of the items based on when they are due to arrive in the store.

To add a delivery schedule:

  1. In the Launcher, expand the Dimensions entry and select Vendors.
  2. In the Vendor window, click on List: the list of vendors opens on the desktop.
  3. Select a vendor.
  4. Press F3 to open the Vendor table.
  5. Click in the delivery section.
    Default values are entered for the Target, Delivery and Cycle fields.
  6.  Edit and add the necessary information.
  7. Click in the delivery section to save the changes.
