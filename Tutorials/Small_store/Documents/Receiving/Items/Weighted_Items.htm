Weighted Items


Receiving weighted items

To add weighted items to a receiving document once the document header is created:

Select  Items  in the  Start  window to open the item search wizard.
 

The  Items into current documents  checkbox is selected by default: only items from Purchase Order will displayed. If there was no Purchase order created prior to the receiving then you will need to uncheck this box.
 

You can receive the weighted items by double-clicking under the receive box under Weight column and entering the value.
 

You can also receive the weighted items by selecting a record from the grid and then clicking on the "Receive" option at the bottom of the screen.
It will bring up the transaction line window where you can enter the weighted value.
