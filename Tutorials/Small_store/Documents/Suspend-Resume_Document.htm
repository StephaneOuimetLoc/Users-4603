Suspend/Resume Document

 An incomplete receiving document may be suspended and edited at a later time.

To suspend a document:

From the Context Tools, click on the hamburger icon  and select "Suspend the document"

The Current Transaction under the Context Tools will be blank.

  To resume or re-open a suspended document:   

  1. Click on Find and Find document in order to search for the receiving document

2. Fill in the information in the Wizard and execute. In this example we're searching for an open receiving document therefore you will need to put a check under "Opened" and "Recv"

From the grid, put a check for the document that you want to open and click on the "Action" menu at the bottom of the screen and select "Open"

Once the document is opened, the Current Transaction under the Context Tools will show the Trs number and the Vendor code
