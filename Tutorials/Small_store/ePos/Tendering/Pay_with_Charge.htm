Information

The charge tender is used when the invoices are charged to customers accounts.

A customer must be entered for each transaction charged to a customer's account. A credit limit limits the amount that can be charged to customers account.

To charge an invoice to a customer's account, please follow the next steps:

  1. At the end of the transaction, select the desired customer, if they are not already selected.
  2. Select the Charge option from the tenders pull-down menu. The "Charge?" message will be displayed in the entry window.
  3. In the entry window, enter the amount charged to the customer's account. The specified amount is charged to the customer's account.

To complete the transaction, the balance must be $0.00. The credit limit of the customer's account is defined in the Risk level table (which is linked to the customer table.) If the customer has no credit, or if their credit limit is reached, the "Customer over limit" message will be displayed in the entry window.
