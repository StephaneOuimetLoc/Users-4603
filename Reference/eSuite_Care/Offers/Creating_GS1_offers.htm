eSuite Care - GS1 Coupon Table

eSuite Care > Offers

Information

The GS1 Databar offers are store and vendor coupons given to specific customers for the purchase of particular items.

GS1 Databar offers do not exist in the system; they are created following the GS1 Databar coupon barcode rules and assigned to a single customer or to multiple customers using the Link offer table.

GS1 offers require the Bank_SMSPayment option.

Displaying the GS1 coupon table

In the Launcher, click the Offers entry:

  1. The Start window opens in the Offer work area.
  2. Click on All: the list of offers opens in the Offers - Items grid.
  3. Select a record and click on Assign offer on the bottom of the item grid.
  4. In the Link Offer table, click Create GS1 coupon: the Create GS1 coupon table opens overlapping the work area.
