Shopper Level Upgrade

eSuite > Customers > Master > General List

Information

The Shopper level table allows customers to upgrade to another shopper level in particular situations. This is useful for special promotions (discounts, more points, add-on amounts on vendor coupons, etc.) to customers when certain conditions are reached. This can be a good way of creating a certain movement in the store on slow moments.

NOTES:
  • The shopper level upgrade is valid for the current transaction only. The conditions are revaluated for each transaction.
  • The shopper level upgrade is performed when the Sub-total function is called in the transaction, so only one upgrade can be performed. It is not possible to upgrade a second time. For example, it is not possible to upgrade from shopper level 1 to shopper level 2 when the conditions are reached and immediately to shopper level 3 if the conditions of the shopper level 2 are also reached. Only the upgrade to shopper level 2 will be performed.

Conditions

The customers automatically upgrade to the shopper level specified in the Upgrade to level field as soon as the conditions are reached. The conditions are based on:

  • Transaction total over an amount
  • Points balance over a number
  • Spending over a certain period
  • Days and time

The conditions can be mixed. In this case, all conditions defined must be reached for the shopper level upgrade to occur.

Transaction total over an amount

The shopper level upgrades to the shopper level defined in the Upgrade to level field if the total of the transaction is equal to or higher than the amount defined in the Transaction total over field.

The totalizer 3 (Net sales) is used by default if no totalizer is defined in the Totalizer field.

To upgrade to another shopper level based on a minimum amount needed in the transaction:

  1. In the desired shopper level, enter the number of the shopper level to upgrade to in the Upgrade to level field.
  2. Type the minimum amount to reach in the Transaction total over field of the same shopper level.
    The amount must be a numeric value, and must contain a decimal point as the dollars/cents separator. There is no fixed limit maximum amount.
  3. Enter the totalizer number to use in the Totalizer field if it is different than 3 (Net sales).

Point balance over a number

The shopper level upgrades to the shopper level defined in the Upgrade to level field if the customer's point balance is equal to or higher than the number of points defined in the Points balance over field.

For the customer's point balance to be considered, a customer must be entered in the transaction.

To upgrade to another shopper level based on a minimum number of points in the customer's point balance:

  1. In the desired shopper level, enter the number of the shopper level to upgrade to in the Upgrade to level field.
  2. Type the minimum number of points needed in the Points balance over field of the same shopper level.

Spending over a certain period

The shopper level upgrades to the shopper level defined in the Upgrade to level field if the total amount of the purchases done for the customer during the period defined in the Period field is equal to or higher than the amount defined in the Period spending field.

The totalizer 3415 (Admissible spending) is used by default if no totalizer is defined in the Totalizer field.

For the customer's total purchase amount to be considered, a customer must be entered in the transaction.

To upgrade to another shopper level based on a minimum amount of purchases done over a period:

  1. In the desired shopper level, enter the number of the shopper level to upgrade to in the Upgrade to level field.
  2. Type the minimum amount to reach in the Period spending field of the same shopper level.
    The amount must be a numeric value and must contain a decimal point as the dollars/cents separator. There is no fixed limit maximum amount.
  3. Enter the letter of the desired period in the Period field.T is for Current transaction.
    • D is for Current day
    • W is for Current week
    • M is for Current month
    • Y is for Current year
    • N is for Cumulative (NRT)
  4. Enter the totalizer number to use in the Totalizer field if it is different than 3415 (Admissible spending).

Days and time

The shopper level upgrades to the shopper level defined in the Upgrade to level field if the transaction is performed over a period of the day defined by the starting and ending hours. The starting and ending hours in the Start and Stop fields are inclusive.

To upgrade to another shopper level based on the time of the day:

  1. In the desired shopper level, enter the number of the shopper level to upgrade to in the Upgrade to level field.
  2. Enter the starting hours of the periods in the Start field of the corresponding days of the same shopper level, in the hhmm format (24-hour).
  3. Enter the ending hours of the periods in the Stop field of the same days, in the hhmm format (24-hour).

For more information on the shopper level upgrades, see the Preferred shopper - Shopper level upgrade - Examples topic.
