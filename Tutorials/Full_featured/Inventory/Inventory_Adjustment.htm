Inventory Adjustment

How to perform an Inventory Adjustment

1.  Expand Inventory in the Launcher from the BUY module

    

2.  Select Adjustment.

  

3.  Click New.

  

4.  Choose Adjustment date and click Create.

  

5.  This will bring you back on the Adjustment Start widget.

6.  Select Items or All to bring up your items in the grid

    

7.  Enter the amount Units in the Quantity column per item. 

  

8.  Press Enter or the Save icon .

9.  If all adjustments are done, close document.
