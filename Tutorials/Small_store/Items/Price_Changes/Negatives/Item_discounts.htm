Information

You can configure discounts directly on a item in the Price Table.

From the launcher in eSuite Care, Manage, Buy, Sell or Batch, click the down arrow in the Items dimension and select Master to access Items start menu.

 

Click on Master , the Items Start window open, then select Items to load the Items-general list wizard.


Setup an Item discount


  1. In the Items General list wizard, use the fields to search for the item you wish to add a discount, then click on execute.
  3. Select your Item by checking the Main code box.
  4. Toggle to the bottom and click on Edit to open a menu.
  5. From the Menu, select Master tables.
  6. The Master tables will open a menu, select Price master.
  7. In the Price Table, you will go to the Dsc $|% field. Enter a dollar amount discount or percentage discount directly on the item.
  8. Click on the check mark to save your discount.
