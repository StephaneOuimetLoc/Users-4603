eSuite - Customer Reports

eSuite > Customers > Master

Information

Customer reports give an overview of customer information such as sales, customer account details and profit movement for evaluation and transaction analysis. They are available from the eSuite's common modules.

To access the customer reports:

  1. In the launcher, click on Customers.
  2. Click on Master: the Start window opens, displaying the customer-related options.
  3. Select the right type of report.
Customer reports:
  • Single total
  • Multi totals
  • Compare totals
  • Transaction analysis
  • Item totals
  • Discount analysis
  • Profitability
NOTES:
  • The General list option provides access to the list of customers matching the filters set in the wizard.
  • The New option offers the tools for the creation of a new customer account.

Single total

The Single total wizard offers criteria selection that includes period, date range, totalizer and target. It allows breaking down the information by date, by store and only customers with no movement. Criteria related to movement, points, account and marketing may be added with the Add criteria button.

The Single total report displays one sum for all the selected totalizers together affecting customers.

In the bottom menu:

  • Edit provides access to all customer tables and allows editing (F3) the column selected in the grid.
  • Analyze allows breaking down information by store or by date, executing the multi totals report, displaying transaction analysis and performing subqueries and linked queries.
  • Action allows assigning offers, adjusting points, displaying offer details and sending notifications.

Compare totals

The Compare totals wizard offers criteria selection that includes period, target, target compare, totalizer and totalizer compare. Criteria related to movement, points, account and marketing may be added with the Add criteria button.

The Compare totals report displays transaction quantities and total sales from a store comparing to another and/or a totalizer compare to another by customer.

In the bottom menu:

  • Edit provides access to all customer tables and allows editing (F3) the column selected in the grid.
  • Analyze allows breaking down information by store or by date, displaying transaction analysis and performing subqueries and linked queries.
  • Action allows assigning offers, adjusting points, displaying offer details and sending notifications.

Transaction analysis

The Transaction analysis wizard offers criteria selection that includes period, totalizer range and target. It allows breaking down the information by date, by store, by operator and by transaction. Criteria related to movement, transaction, account and marketing may be added with the Add criteria button.

The Transaction analysis report displays the details of each transaction such as date, customer name, the selected totalizer, the item quantity and its value.

In the bottom menu:

  • Edit provides access to all customer tables and allows editing (F3) the column selected in the grid.
  • Analyze allows viewing the electronic journal detail and total, displaying transaction analysis and performing subqueries and linked queries.

Criteria related to movement, transaction, account and marketing may be added with the Add criteria button.

The Item total report displays the total of items and the total sales by customer.

In the bottom menu:

Discount analysis

The Discount analysis wizard offers criteria selection that includes period and target. It allows breaking down the information by date, by store and by customer. Criteria related to movement, discount, account and marketing may be added with the Add criteria button.

The Discount analysis report displays the value of each discount such as item discount, coupon discount and manual discount applied on transactions.

In the bottom menu:

  • Edit provides access to all customer tables and allows editing (F3) the column selected in the grid.
  • Analyze allows breaking down information by date, by store or by customer, displaying transaction analysis and performing subqueries and linked queries.

Profitability

The Profitability wizard offers criteria selection that includes period and target. It allows breaking down the information by date and by store. Criteria related to movement, account and marketing may be added with the Add criteria button.

The Profitability report displays profit information by customer.

In the bottom menu:

  • Edit provides access to all customer tables and allows editing (F3) the column selected in the grid.
  • Analyze allows displaying transaction analysis and performing subqueries and linked queries.
