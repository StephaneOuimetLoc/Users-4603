eSuite - Reports Overview

eSuite General

Information

The eSuite software provides the tools for reporting on all aspects of retail management, including total sales, operator balancing, sales by item, vendor, department, etc.

Every time the eSuite software "polls" the sales workstations, it stores the sales information in database tables. The daily figures are used to calculate weekly, monthly and yearly cumulative totals for all totalizers, which are also stored in the database. Because the cumulative totals are generated and stored, weekly or monthly reports do not take longer to generate.

The common modules of eSuite cover the following reporting areas: customers, dimensions and items.

Access the customer reports:

  1. Click the Customers entry in the launcher.
  2. Select Master: the Start window opens in the work area, displaying all types of customer-related reports.

Access the dimensions reports:

  1. Click the Dimensions entry in the launcher.
  2. Select the dimension for the desired report among Financials, Departments, Sub-departments, Categories, Families, Report codes, Vendors and Hourly.
    For each of these dimensions, the Start window opens in the work area, displaying all types of dimension-related reports.

Access the item reports:

  1. Click the Items entry in the launcher.
  2. Select Master: the Start window opens in the work area, displaying all types of item-related reports.

Other types of reports are available from their specific module:

  • eSuite Care: KPI, segmentation, campaigns, offers, points
  • eSuite Manage: financials, operators, balancing, accounting

For details on how to generate reports, see the Generating reports topic.
