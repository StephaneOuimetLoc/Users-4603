eSuite Care - Campaign Reports

eSuite Care > Campaigns

Information

The Campaigns reports display an overview of the overall success of the campaign. The aim is to determine whether it has achieved its intended impact and if it is worth investing in such a campaign. Campaign batches should be composed of items on sale or coupons that are only active for the duration of the batch. That allows the report to run only for these items and only for the duration of the campaign.

The Campaigns reports are available from eSuite Care:

  1. In the launcher, click on Campaigns.
  2. Select the right type of report from the Start window.
 

Campaign reports:

  • List
  • Result
  • Impact

Campaign list

The Campaign list displays the all the campaigns (old, active and future batches), with their active and ending dates.

In the bottom menu:

  • Edit provides access to the Batch header table, for batches that have not been executed.

Campaign result

The Campaign result wizard offers criteria selection that includes batch, target and totalizer range. The date range is added when the Use open dates box is checked. Information can be displayed by date, store and item.

A batch containing coupons or items on sale must be selected in the Batch number field to execute the report.

The Campaign result report displays the sum of the selected totalizers of every item found in a batch (campaign) during active period of the the campaign.

In the bottom menu:

  • Analyze breaks down the report information information by date, store and item.

Below is a brief description of the information displayed by default in the Campaign result report.

COLUMN DESCRIPTION
Sum
Quantity The total quantity of items sold during the campaign.
Amount The total amount of net sales for the items sold during the campaign.
Weight The total weight of the items sold during the campaign.

NOTE: This report requires additional configurations to function. See your reseller for details.

Campaign impact

The Campaign impact wizard offers criteria selection that includes batch, target and totalizer range. The date range is added when the Use open dates box is checked. Additional criteria related to dates, account, marketing and items may be added with the Add criteria button. Only items in the selected batch are displayed when Use only items from batch case is checked. Information may be displayed by date, by store and by item.

A batch containing coupons or items on sale must be selected in the Batch number field to execute the report.

The Campaign impact report displays the sum of selected totalizers over three periods based on the batch (campaign) dates. By default, it uses the same period before and after the batch.

In the bottom menu:

  • Analyze breaks down the information by item, store and customer.

The periods before and after the campaign have the same length as the campaign period for comparison purposes. This report only displays transactions having a customer account number.

Below is a brief description of the information displayed by default in the Campaign impact report.

COLUMN DESCRIPTION
Store ID The store number.
Visit count
Before campaign The number of visits before the campaign.
During campaign The number of visits during the campaign.
After campaign The number of visits after the campaign.
Variance after campaign The difference in percentage between the number of visits during the campaign and the number of visits after the campaign.
Amount sum
Before campaign The total sales before the campaign.
During campaign The total sales during the campaign.
After campaign The total sales after the campaign.
Variance after campaign The difference in percentage between the total sales during the campaign and the total sales after the campaign.

Note:
This report requires additional configurations to function. See your reseller for details.
