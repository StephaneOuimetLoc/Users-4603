mFloor - Invoicing & Line Buster

mFloor Selling

Information

This document describes how to work with the Invoicing module and perform the following tasks:

  • Creating and adding items to a new invoice
  • Opening a previously suspended invoice document
  • Closing a invoice document to issue to the customer
  • Using the main invoicing application menu

Line busting with mFloor

Line busting is a retail approach in which floor clerks can ring up customers' purchases directly on the floor with a mobile device or tablet and have them pay for their purchases at one of the POS workstations. In mFloor, an Invoice can be created and suspended, and then recalled at a cash register ensuring that customers can pay quickly and efficiently, providing a new service for the store.

Creating and adding items to a new invoice

To create and add items to a invoice the items must be associated to a vendor through the cost table, which must be done in SMS. If the items to add to the purchase order are already associated with a vendor, proceed below to creating a new purchase order.

To create a new invoice for a customer follow the steps below:

  1. The invoice menu opens on the Open invoice screen. If any open invoices already exist they are displayed in upper section of the screen (A).
  2. To add a new invoice tap or click (1) in the upper right had corner.
  3. The new invoice screen appears displaying a list of customers(B). To scroll through the list of customers swipe the screen or if using a mouse, click and drag on the list.
  4. Tap or click a customer name to choose it.
  5. Once a customer is chosen the invoice document header appears displaying the customer name, order number, and associated dates.
  6. To add items to the new invoice:
    1. Type the item code in the Enter item code box at the top of the screen.

      or
    2. Tap or click and scan the item code

      or
    3. Tap or click and a list of items for the vendor appears and choose an item by tapping or clicking it. In the case of a long list swipe or click and drag to scroll the list.
    4. At the item screen enter the quantity to sell in the Unit box and tap or click <ENTER>
  7. Scan the next item or Enter the code in Enter item code or click to go back and choose another item from Quick search.
  8. Repeat all of the above steps (a-e) to add items to the invoice.

Opening a previously suspended invoice

When working with invoice documents users can suspend documents using the application menu or documents are automatically suspended when users tap or click when leaving a invoice.

To open a previously suspended invoice document follow the steps below:

  1. If not already in the mFloor application, open the mFloor application and log in.
  2. At the main menu of the mFloor application tap or click Selling module.
  3. At the Selling module main menu click Invoicing & Line buster.
  4. The Open invoice menu appears showing open/suspended invoices, if the list is long, swipe or click and scroll to view all orders.
  5. Tap or click on the desired invoice to open it.

Closing a invoice document to issue to the customer

Closing an invoice document removes it from the list of open documents displayed in the Open invoice screen in mFloor.

To close a invoice document do the following steps below:

  1. If not already done, select the invoice to be closed from the list of open invoices as explained above.
  2. Once the invoice is opened, tap or click the application menu and then tap or click Invoice Total.
  3. mFloor will return to the Invoice menu.

Using the invoice application menu

When a user is in a invoice an icon appears for the invoice application menu in the top right hand corner of the screen.  

To perform the actions described below on an invoice tap or a click on and then tap or click the associated menu action.

  • To show items in the current invoice tap or click List line items.
  • To show the invoice header tap or click Invoice Header.
  • To cancel the invoice tap or click Cancel Document.
  • To suspend the invoice tap or click Suspend document.
  • To close and issue the invoice to a customer tap or click Invoice Total.
