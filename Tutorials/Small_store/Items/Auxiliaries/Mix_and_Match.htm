eSuite - Mix & Match Table

eSuite > Items > Item auxialaries > Mix & Matches

Table of Contents

Information

The Mix & Match table contains entries that allow you to expand a package price, like 3/$6.00, across multiple skus.  For example, if you want to sell a combination of pop and chips at 3/6.00 you can assign a mix and match so your customer can buy, 1 orange pop, 1 grape pop and a bag of chips pay $6.00 verus the client having to buy 3 of each to get a package price special.

NOTE: The mix & match records do not contain or keep track of quantities, amounts, discounts or mix & match types. They are only used to link items to mix them and then match them to the price defined in the Price table.


Displaying the Mix & Match Table

From the launcher in eSuite Care, Manage, Buy or Sell click the down arrow in the Items entry and select Item auxiliaries to display the Start wizard

 

Note: In the Batch module, Item auxiliaries is in the main menu, not nested under Items

Select Mix & Matches to display grid list

Click on  in the grid toolbar to display the Mix & Match Edit table

To create a Mix & Match

1. Display the Mix & Match Edit table as above or click on  in the tool bar of a populated edit table

2. Enter a number and a descriptor

3. To save, click the  in the toolbar or hit enter on your keyboard

4. To review your entry, select  to refresh the grid 

To modify a Mix & Match

1. From the Mix & Match grid, select/highlight the entry you wish to edit

2. Double click on the entry, press F3 on your keyboard or click on Edit from the bottom grid menu.

3. The edit table will open

4. Modify as required

5. Save

Applying a Mix & Match

Once created, the corresponding mix & match number is assigned to the Mix field in the Price Edit table.  You can enter the Mix & Match number or select the dropdown to choose a listing.

Note: If you re-assign a mix and match number, ensure it has been removed from the previous group of items in the price table or they will be come part of the new group.
