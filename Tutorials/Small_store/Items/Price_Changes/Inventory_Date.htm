Inventory Date

Change The Global Date in eSuite

1.  From the BUY module launcher.

2.  Expand Tools.

3.  Expand Configure.

4.  Select Global Date.

 

5.  You will be prompted to enter the date in MMDDYYYY format.

6.  Press Enter to confirm.

You will see a popup message on the bottom right confirming the date change has been successful.
