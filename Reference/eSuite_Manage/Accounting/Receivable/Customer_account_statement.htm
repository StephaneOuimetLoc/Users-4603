This document explains how the Customer account statement works.

Make sure that the information in the account statement report is displayed. This is done by the Calculate cumulatives command in the Scheduler, which must be executed on a daily basis.

Example:

  1. The customer has a starting balance of $0.00.
  2. The account is charged $50.00 in a transaction.
