Delete a customer account    

eSuite > Customer > Master

Table of content

Information

The eSuite software offers the tools for deleting customer accounts

WARNING: When a customer is deleted, it is impossible to extract the historical information even if the information exists in the database. Consider modifying existing records rather than deleting them

From the launcher in eSuite Care, Manage, Buy, Sell or Batch, click the down arrow in the Customer entry to display the Customer menu.

Click on Master: the Start window opens for Customers.

  1. Click on General list
  2. Enter the customer information to be able to find the customer account to be deleted.
  3. Click Execute: the results displays in Customers - general list.
  4. In the bottom menu of the list, click the Edit button to display its menu
  5. Select the Customer option: the Customer table opens, displaying the information on the selected customer
  6. Click Delete in the toolbar: a confirmation window is displayed.
    .
  7. Click Yes: the Customer table closes
  8. Click Refresh in the bottom menu of the customer grid to refresh the view: the customer account that has just been deleted will not be displayed anymore
