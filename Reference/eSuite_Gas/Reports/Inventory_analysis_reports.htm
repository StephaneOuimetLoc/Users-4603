eSuite Gas - Inventory Analysis Reports

eSuite Gas > Gas > Inventory Analysis

Information

Inventory analysis reports give an overview of multiple information such as delivery and inventory and sales blending. Report can be execute from Store  and from the Host.

To access the inventory analysis reports:

  1. In the launcher, click on Gas.
  2. Click on Inventory analysis: the Start window opens, displaying the inventory-related options.
  3. Select the right type of report.
Inventory analysis reports:
  • Delivery and inventory
  • Sales blending

Delivery and inventory

The Delivery and inventory wizard offers criteria selection that includes period, date range and target. It allows breaking down the information by date and by store. Report  can be execute from the Store and from the Host.

The Inventory analysis report displays amount and volume for previous inventory, sales, delivery and end inventory for each tank.

In the bottom menu:

  • Analyze allows breaking down information by store, by date or blending sales, displaying inventory analysis and performing subqueries and linked queries.

Sales blending

The Sales blending wizard offers criteria selection that includes period, date range and target. It allows breaking down the information by date and by store.

The Sales blending report displays detailed information on price, amount, volume, etc. for the selected items and tanks.

In the bottom menu:

  • Analyze allows breaking down information by store or by date and performing subqueries and linked queries.
