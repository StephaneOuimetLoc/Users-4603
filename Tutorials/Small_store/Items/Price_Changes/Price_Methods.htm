Quick Price Change

It is faster and easier than ever to change a price with eSuite editable grid design. From any eSuite application, click on Items/Master 1 . This will display the Item widget below:

Then click on the on Prices button 2  . This will take you to the wizard below:

   

To  locate the desired item, enter the item number in the Any code field 1 and press Enter or click Execute 2 . Upon execution, the Price grid will show the result of your search:

The cursor will automatically be on the Price column 1 so you can start typing the price and press Enter when complete. You can also click on the Post button 2 :

Your price change is done! Now the last step is to deploy the changes to POS.
