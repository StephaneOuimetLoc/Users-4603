eSuite - How to Initialize the Perpetual Inventory

eSuite > Items                        

Information

When inventory is managed based on perpetual inventory, there are two (2) modes you can set your system: Inventory on every item or After inventory initialization. This configuration is set up in the System.ini. The default mode is Inventory on every item.

After inventory initialization mode is used when you want a perpetual inventory on specific items only, comparatively to the Inventory on every item mode where you have a perpetual inventory on all items.

The inventory initialization may be done for a single item or a group of items .

To initialize a single item

In the launcher, click the arrow in the Items entry to display its menu and then:

  1. Click on Master: the Start window opens in the Items work area.
  2. Click on Item list to open the wizard.
  3. Enter the search criteria.
  4. Click on Execute: results will display in the Items - General list window.
    Select an item from the list and click on Action at bottom of the screen and select Initialize Inventory
   

A confirmantion message will appear where you will need to select Yes.

 In case the item is being used in an open document then an error will appear requesting to close the document.

Once the initialization is complete then a conifrmation message will appear stating that it was done.

To initialize a group of items

In the launcher, click the arrow in the Items entry to display its menu and then:

  1. Click on Master: the Start window opens in the Items work area.
  2. Click on Item list to open the wizard.
  3. Enter the search criteria in the Wizard, for example sub-dept or/and add More criteria
  4. Click on Execute: results will display in the Items - General list window.

Select all items and click on Action at bottom of the screen then select Intialize Inventory

You will get confirmation on the number of records that will be initialized

Once complete then you will get confirmation that it was done.
