eSuite Care - Offers Overview

eSuite Care > Offers

Information

Offers are store and vendor coupons that are given by the system when the specified conditions are reached. They can be for specific customers or they may have to be clipped from the store website.

Offers in eSuite

The Offers entry is available in the eSuite Care module. The options related to offers are grouped in the Start window:

The following options are available:

  • Targeted offers opens the wizard that enables searching for targeted offers. It gives access to the Offers - Items grid.
  • All (offer items) opens the list of all offers.
  • Customers opens the wizard that enables searching for or creating customers and displaying customer-related information.
  • Assigned offers opens the wizard that enables searching for assigned offers. It gives access to the Offers - Assigned report.
  • All (assigned offers) opens the list of all assigned offers.
  • Details opens the wizard that enables searching for details of the assigned offers. It gives access to the Offers - Details grid.
  • Redemptions opens the wizard that enables searching for total discounts and total sales. It gives access to the Offers - Redemptions report.
  • Offer tasks opens the list of all scheduled tasks associated to offers.

For more details, see the Offers - reports topic.
