eSuite Manage - Safe

eSuite Manage > Balancing > Drawer/Safe

Table of Contents

Information

Actions available when performing Drawer operations.

  1. In the launcher, click on Balancing > Drawer/Safe: the Start window opens, displaying the Safe-related options.
  2. Select the right type of Safe action.

 

    Safe Actions:

  • Content
  • Receive
  • Deposit
  • Declare
  • Inventory
  • History


Safe Content

Information:

The Safe Content wizard offers a criteria selection that includes Balancing period, date ,Tender, and More criteria. It allows breaking down the information by operator and by tender and showing only totalizers with no movement. Criteria related to movement may be added by selecting it in the wizard page.

Note: You also have the option to add other totalizers by selecting the More Criteria button.

Safe Content Grid

 The safe content grid will populate the Tender (broken down by number and description) , and Amount (broken down by Previous Loan, Pickup, Received, Deposit, Expected, Undeclared, declared and Over/Short). The content grid also gives you the option to perform a Receive, Deposit and/or a Declare all in one place.

Safe Receive

Information:

The  Safe Receive  function is used to track the media/tender types that are deposited into the safe. So, each time money is received from the financial institution (not from the pickups), the safe total must be corrected. Each received amount is posted to the database and added to the safe total.

The Wizard offers criteria selection that includes Balancing period, Date, Tender, and More Criteria It allows breaking down the information by operator, amount and by tender and showing only totalizers with no movement. Criteria related to movement may be added by selecting it in the wizard page.

Note: You also have the option to add other totalizers by selecting the More Criteria button.

Safe Receive Grid

 The safe receive grid will populate the Tender (broken down by number and description) and Amount (broken down by Safe, New Receive, Total receive, New Safe).

You can either double click on New receive entry or manually enter an amount (after manually entering in an amount, hit the enter key and then save)

Once you double click on the New receive entry,  the Cash calculator opens up where it is is possible to either enter a manual amount or break it down by denomination.

Note: The Safe - Receive grid gives you the option to view the Safe-receive history.

The Cash calculator allows you to enter a manual amount or break the amount down by Denomination.

Note: Only the Cash tender and Foreign currency will open up the Cash / Foreign currency deposit window where you can either enter a manual amount or break it down by denominator.  Any other tender will be grayed out.

 

Note: The Context tools will give you a summary of the transactions that have been made during the session..

Safe Deposit

Information

The Deposit function is used during the business day to track the media/tender type removed from the from the safe to deposit with a financial institution. Each deposited amount is posted to the database and subtracted from the safe total. The safe total must be corrected every time a deposit is made.

The Safe Deposit wizard offers criteria selection that includes Balancing period, date,  operator,  tender, and more criteria. It allows breaking down the information by operator, amount and by tender and showing only totalizers with no movement. Criteria related to movement may be added by selecting it in the wizard page.

Safe Deposit Grid

 The Safe Deposit grid will populate the Tender (broken down by number and description) and Amount (broken down by Safe, New Deposit, Total Deposit, New Safe.

You can either double click on New deposit or manually enter an amount.

Once you double click on the new deposit entry,  the Cash calculator opens up where it is is possible to either enter a manual amount or break it down by denomination.

Note: Only the Cash tender and Foreign currency will open up the Cash / Foreign currency deposit window where you can either enter a manual amount or break it down by denominator.  Any other tender will be grayed out.

Safe Declare

InformationInformation

The  Safe Declare  function is used to clear out the safe totals.

The Safe Declare wizard offers criteria selection that includes Balancing period, date,  tender, and more criteria. It allows breaking down the information by operator, amount and by tender and showing only totalizers with no movement. Criteria related to movement may be added by selecting it in the wizard page.

Safe Declare Grid

 The Safe Declare grid will populate the Tender (broken down by number and description) and Amount (broken down by Safe, New Declare, Total Declare and Short/over.

Note: To view if there are any undeclared amounts, you will need to verify in the Safe Content Grid.

To declare an amount, you will need to do the following:-

  • Double-click on New declare and enter the amounts that need to be declared in the denomination window.
  • Click execute, then save the changes.

Note: The amounts that were entered by the user will be populated under the Total declare field, but won't be saved until you save the changes from the Declare Grid.

Note: The context menu displays the Session information so the user always knows which operator is doing the transaction and the global date that applies.

Safe Inventory

formationInformation

The Safe inventory report provides with a list of all movements in / out of each safe type broken down by denomination (loan, pickup, received, deposit) as well as the safe's current value.

The Safe Inventory wizard offers criteria selection that includes Balancing period, date,  Safes Currency and more criteria. It allows breaking down the information by Totalizers, amount and by tender and showing only totalizers with no movement. Criteria related to movement may be added by selecting it in the wizard page.

Safe Inventory Grid

 The Safe Inventory grid will populate the Totalizers (broken down description) and Open Safe (broken down by Plus , Minus and Balance.

Safe History

Information:

In the eSuite Balancing menu, you have the safe history screen that provides you with a list of withdrawals and receiving that were performed by period (day, week, Month etc...).

The Safe History wizard offers criteria selection that includes Period, Date range, Tender, Type, Target and More Criteria. It allows breaking down the information by operator, amount and by tender and showing only totalizers with no movement. Criteria related to movement may be added by selecting it in the wizard page.

Safe History Grid

 The Safe History grid will populate the Date, Store ID, Terminal, Transaction, Time and Type) , Tender (broken down by number and description) and Amount.
