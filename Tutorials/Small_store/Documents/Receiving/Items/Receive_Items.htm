Receive Items

  

Adding items to the receiving document

To add items to a receiving document once the document header is created:

Select  Items  in the  Start  window to open the item search wizard.

 


The  Items into current documents  checkbox is selected by default: only items from Purchase Order will displayed. If there was no Purchase order created prior to the receiving then you will need to uncheck this box.
 

Enter the item or a range of item(s) and click  Execute or leave all fields blank to retrieve all items for the selected vendor.

A list of items opens on the desktop.

Depending on the Unit of measure, you can receive the cases or units by double-clicking under the receive box and entering the value.
The Total column is also an editable field.
 


You can also receive items by selecting a record from the grid and then clicking on the "Receive" option at the bottom of the screen.
It will bring up the transaction line window where you can unter the receipt values.
