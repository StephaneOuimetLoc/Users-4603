eSuite - Customers Overview

eSuite > Customers

Information

The Customers entry is common to both the Care and the Manage modules. It contains the main and the auxiliary information related to customers. The customers maintenance can be carried out by managing records directly from the database.

The eSuite software keeps the customer information in different grids and tables that are accessible from the Customers drop-down menu in the launcher:

The Customers entry is split in two sub-entries that are linked to each other:

  • Master, giving access to the main customer information, and
  • Customer auxiliaries, containing additional customer information.

Master

The Master sub-entry opens a list of options allowing the search for customer-related information:

The New option is used for creating new customer accounts. The other options in the Start window give access to the customer-related tables and allow searches for information related to customers, such as details on customer accounts, sales, profit movement, etc.
