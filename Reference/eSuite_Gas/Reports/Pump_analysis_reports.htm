eSuite Gas - Pump Analysis Reports

eSuite > Gas > Pump Analysis

Information

Pump analysis reports give an overview of multiple information such as sales analysis, sales by grade, sales by hose and sales by transaction. Report can be execute from Store  and from the Host.

To access the pump analysis reports:

  1. In the launcher, click on Gas.
  2. Click on Pump analysis: the Start window opens, displaying the pump-related options.
  3. Select the right type of report.
Pump analysis reports:
  • Sales analysis
  • Sales by grade
  • Sales by grade
  • Sales by transaction
  • Baskey

Sales analysis

The Sales analysis wizard offers criteria selection that includes period, date range and target. It allows breaking down the information by date and by store.

The Sales Analysis report displays amount and volume for all sub-department related to pump.

In the bottom menu:

  • Analyze allows breaking down information by store, by date or sales by grade, displaying transaction analysis and performing subqueries and linked queries.

Sales by grades

The Sales by grades wizard offers criteria selection that includes period, date range and target. It allows breaking down the information by date, by store.

The Sales by grades report displays details for all the selected item code sush has amount and volume for end counter, start counter, total, POS total, variance.

In the bottom menu:

  • Analyze allows breaking down information by store or by date, displaying transaction analysis and performing subqueries and linked queries.

Back to top

Sales by hoses

The Sales by hoses wizard offers criteria selection that includes period, date range and target.

The Sale by hoses report displays fuel item code, hose id, volume %, price, amount and volume.

In the bottom menu:

  • Analyze allows breaking down information by store or by date, displaying transaction analysis and performing subqueries and linked queries.

Back to top

Sales by transaction

The Transaction analysis wizard offers criteria selection that includes period, date range and target. It allows breaking down the information by date, by store.

The Sales by transaction report displays the details of each transaction such as terminal ID, item code, hose ID, time, sale type, transaction number, mode, price, discount, amount, volume.
