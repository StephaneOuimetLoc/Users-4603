eSuite Buy - How to Manage Billing Documents

eSuite Buy > Buy Bill

Information

The eSuite Buy module offers the tools for managing the billing of the merchandise.

This document shows how to:

  • Create and edit a billing document.
  • Add items.
  • Remove items.
  • Add taxes and fees.
  • Subtract from the billing document.
  • Charge to the account.
  • Manage quantities.
  • Suspend or close the billing document.
  • Delete a billing document.

Creating a billing document

New billing documents are created from the closed receiving when merchandise has been ordered and received. When there is no closed receiving document, the new billing documents must be created from scratch.

 To create a billing document from a closed receiving:

  1. In the launcher, click the arrow in the Buy entry to display its menu.
  2. Click on Bill: the Start window opens on the desktop.
  3. Click All next to Documents: the Open document grid opens on the desktop, displaying the billing documents.
  4. Select the closed receiving document and right-click on it to display the available options.
  5. Click on Open to confirm the creation of the billing document.

To create a billing document from scratch:

  1. In the launcher, click the arrow in the Buy entry to display its menu.
  2. Click on Bill: the Start window opens on the desktop.
  3. Click on New: the New document window opens on the desktop.
  4. Select a vendor from the Vendor field.
  5. Click on Create to confirm the creation of a new billing document for the selected vendor.

When a billing document is created:

  • It is listed in the Open document grid.
  • Its status is OPEN.
  • The Document (full) option in the start menu is enabled.
    This gives access to the Document window with information on the document and tools to edit it.

Adding items to the billing document

Items may only be added to billing documents created from scratch.

Steps:

  1. Select Items in the Start window to open the item search wizard.
  2. Add all necessary criteria and click Execute: the list of items opens on the desktop.

    In the document:
    • The Amount, Case, Unit, Weight and Cost fields in the Document column are editable.
  3. To edit the billed quantities, amounts and costs, double-click in the corresponding field and add the new values.
    Press Enter after each new value.

  4. Click on Save in the toolbar: the Line and Quantity fields are displayed in the Document column.

Editing the billing document

Before closing the bill, you may want to revise and edit the information in it. This is only possible for billing documents that have been created from scratch (no purchase order available).

Billing information is available in the Document window.

Steps:

  1. From the start menu, click on Documents - All: the list of billing documents opens in the Open document grid.
  2. Select the document you want to edit information for.
  3. Right-click to display the list of options.
  4. Click on Open in the menu.
  5. Right-click again on the billing document and select Show details: the Document window opens on the desktop.

The Document window of an open billing is also available from the Document (full) option in the start menu.

For details on the fields and functionalities, see Document window.

Editing general information

General information related to vendor and shipping may be edited in the Transaction header table.

  1. From the Document window, click on the Edit header button in the upper part of the document.
    The Transaction header table opens on the desktop.

  2. Make all necessary changes.
  3. Click on Apply changes in the toolbar.

For details on the fields and functionalities, see Transaction header table.

Editing specific information

Information related to items may be edited in the Transaction line bill table. This is only possible for billing documents that have been created from scratch.

  1. From the Document window, select an item in the Lines section.
  2. Click on Edit selected line in the upper part of the section.
    The Transaction line bill table opens on the desktop.

  3. Make all the necessary changes.
  4. Click on Apply changes in the toolbar.

For details on the fields and functionalities, see Transaction line bill table.

Editing lines

Quantities (cases, units, weight) and amounts of a billing document created from scratch may be modified directly in the Lines section.

  1. Double-click in the case, unit, weight or amounts column of the item.
  2. Enter or select the new value.
  3. If necessary, modify the amount.

  4. Click Save all changes in the toolbar of the line section.

Voiding items

Items may be removed from a billing document created from scratch.

  1. In the Line section of the Document window, select the item to remove.
  2. Right-click to display the menu.
  3. Click on Void selected line: a confirmation message is prompted.

  4. Click on Yes to delete the item from the billing document.

Adding taxes and fees

The Add to bill feature allows adding different taxes and fees to the billing document.

  1. From the Document window toolbar, click on to open the Add to bill window.


  2. Make all necessary changes.
  3. Click on Save all changes in the toolbar.

Subtracting from bill

The Subtract from bill feature allows deducting different amounts from the bill.

  1. From the Document window toolbar, click on to open the Subtract from bill window.

  2. Make all necessary changes.
  3. Click on Save all changes in the toolbar.

Charging to the account

The Charge to account feature allows selecting the buyer's account and paying the bill.

  1. From the Document window toolbar, click on  Charge to account: a confirmation message is prompted.

  2. Click on Yes to confirm the payment: the Transaction line charge window opens on the desktop.

  3. Select an account from the Account field.
  4. Click on Save record in the toolbar.
    The balance of the bill is automatically charged to the selected account and the bill amount is brought back to $0.00.
    This information is displayed in the Totals section of the Document window:

Suspending a billing document

An incomplete billing document may be suspended and edited at a later time.

  1. Open the Document window for the billing document.
  2. Click on Suspend the document in the toolbar.
    The Document window closes.

To open a suspended billing document:

  1. From the Open documents grid, right-click on the suspended document.
  2. Select Open from the menu.
    The billing document is available from the Document (full) option in the start menu.

Closing a billing document

Billing documents need to be closed for the billing to be intiated.

  1. From the list of documents, open the billing document.
  2. From the Start window, click on Document (full): the Document window opens on the desktop.
  3. Click on Close document in the window toolbar: a confirmation message is prompted.

  4. Click on Yes to close the order.
  5. In the open order grid, click on Refresh in the bottom menu: the closed billing document is not listed any more.

When a billing document is closed, it is automatically transferred to the list of open documents in the billing.

NOTE: It is not possible to reopen a closed billing document.

Deleting a billing document

Open documents may be deleted from the Document window:

  1. In the toolbar, click on Cancel: a confirmation mesage is prompted:

  2. Click on Yes to delete the billing document.
  3. In the Open document grid, click on Refresh in the bottom menu: the deleted document is not listed any more.
