eSuite - Coupon Edit Table

eSuite > Items> Master > New

Table of content

Information

The Coupon Edit table contains all the nedddddcessary fields to configure a coupon. From the coupon edit table you can access additional information to your coupon configuration, such as: Criteria, Details and Descriptions.

From the launcher in eSuite Care, Manage, Buy, Sell or Batch, toggle down to the Items dimension and select Master. From there you can access the Items Start Menu.

Creating a coupon

Coupon configurations can have many possibilities, here are the basic steps to create a coupon with minimal requirements.

  1. In the Item Start menu you will see : Coupons, All and New, Select New to open a Coupon Edit Table.   
  2. Select the value type of discount your coupon will apply (example: percentage).
  3. Enter the Value of your coupon (example: 10 will represent 10%).
  4. Select the coupon type ( example: electronic).
  5. Name your coupon by adding a coupon description.
  6. Enter a sub-department.     
  7. You can choose the UPC of the coupon or leave it blank and the system will generate a code if blank.     
  8. Click on Criteria to open the add criteria window.
  9. Define your coupon criteria (the criteria will be specific to your requirements).
  10. In the Details section, you will need to define the funding of the coupon ; store or vendor coupon.
  11. To save your coupon , click on the checkmark.
