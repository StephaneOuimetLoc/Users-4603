eSuite - Serial Number

eSuite > Items

Information

The eSuite software can manage serial numbers on items.

To get this feature to work, enter the keyword SERIALNUMBER in the Behavior script type field of the POS table (Advanced tab) for the items for which you want to track the serial number.

Please add where exactly the serial number will be displayed (Raluca, 2019-08-19)

When this item is sold at the POS, the cashier is prompted to enter the serial number.

The serial numbers entered by the cashiers may be retrieved in the Electronic Journal.

NOTE: The serial number feature has been developed for the Selling module only.
