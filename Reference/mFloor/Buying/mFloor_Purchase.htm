mFloor - Purchase Order Menu

mFloor Procurement

Information

This document describes how to work with the Purchase order module and perform the following tasks:

  • Creating and adding items to a new purchase order
  • Opening a previously suspended purchase order document
  • Closing a purchase order document to issue to the vendor
  • Using the main purchase order application menu

Creating and adding items to a new purchase order

To create and add items to a purchase order the items must be associated to a vendor through the cost table, which must be done in SMS. If the items to add to the purchase order are already associated with a vendor, proceed below to creating a new purchase order.

To create a new purchase order on a vendor follow the steps below:

  1. The purchase order menu opens on the Open order screen. If any open purchase orders already exist they are displayed in upper section of the screen (A).
  2. To add a new purchase order tap or click (1) in the upper right had corner.

  3. The new order screen appears displaying a list of vendors(B). To scroll through the list of vendors swipe the screen or if using a mouse, click and drag on the list.
  4. Tap or click a vendor name to choose it.

  5. Once a vendor is chosen the purchase order document header appears displaying the vendor name, order number, and associated dates.
  6. To add items to the new purchase order:
    1. Type the item code in the Enter item code box at the top of the screen.

      or
    2. Tap or click and scan the item code

      or
    3. Tap or click and a list of items for the vendor appears and choose an item by tapping or clicking it. In the case of a long list swipe or click and drag to scroll the list.
    4. At the item screen enter the quantity to order in the Case box and tap or click <ENTER>

    5. Scan the next item or Enter the code in Enter item code or click to go back and choose another item from Quick search.
    6. Repeat all of the above steps (a-e) to add items to the purchase order.

Viewing last three orders quantity

While in the Item page, a section located at the bottom shows the last ordered quantities of this item. It shows the quantity, who did it and when.

Opening a previously suspended purchase order document

When working with purchase order documents users can suspend documents using the application menu or documents are automatically suspended when users tap or click when leaving a purchase order.

To open a previously suspended purchase order document follow the steps below:

  1. If not already in the mFloor application, open the mFloor application and log in.
  2. At the main menu of the mFloor application tap or click Buying module.
  3. At the Buying module main menu click Purchase order
  4. The Open order menu appears showing open/suspended orders, if the list is long, swipe or click and scroll to view all orders.
  5. Tap or click on the desired purchase order to open it.

Closing a purchase order document to issue to the vendor

Closing a purchase order document removes it from the list of open documents seen in the Open order screen in mFloor.

To close a purchase order document do the following steps below:

  1. If not already done, select the purchase order to be closed from the list of open purchase orders as explained above.
  2. Once the purchase order is open To close the purchase order document tap or click the application menu and tap or click Close document.
  3. mFloor returns to the the Purchase order menu.

Using the purchase order application menu

When a user is in a purchase order document an icon appears for the purchase order application menu in the top right hand corner of the screen.  

To perform the actions described below on a purchase order document tap or a click on and then tap or click the associated menu action.

  • To show items in the current purchase order tap or click List line items.
  • To print the purchase order to the default printer tap or click Print report.
  • To void the purchase order tap or click Mark as void.
  • To suspend the purchase order tap or click Suspend document.
  • To close and issue purchase order to vendor tap or click Close document.

Sending PO by email to Vendor

If the Vendor is set with an email in the Vendor table, mFloor will ask the user if the document can be sent by email. The user can choose Yes or No.
