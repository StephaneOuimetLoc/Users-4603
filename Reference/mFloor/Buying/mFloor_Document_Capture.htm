mFloor - Buying Module Document Capture

mFloor Procurement

Information

Using LOC SMS mFloor, is it possible to take pictures of the paperwork and link them to the current LOC SMS document for future reference and backup. This allows to link the physical papers to the electronic order in LOC SMS but can also serve as a backup in case the papers are lost or broken.

This option is available only in the Buying module of LOC SMS mFloor. All three options (Purchase Order, Receiving and Delivery/DSD) have the capability.

Steps to capture

Here are the steps to follow in order to capture the documents:

  1. Open the document in mFloor and stay on the Header page (main).
  2. Press on the top-right button to open the contextual menu and select Bill and receipt pictures.
  3. On the bottom section, select a picture stored on the device (Choose File button) or take a new picture using the device's camera.
  4. Choose the first page picture or take a picture of the first page.
  5. Once done, press the + button on the top-right corner.
  6. Press the Blue Arrow button to refresh the current page and see the taken picture.
  7. Repeat steps 3 to 5 to take pictures of the subsequent pages.
From Header menu, press on Bill and receipt pictures.
  1. Choose or take picture
  2. Press the Add button
  3. Refresh the page to see the new picture added.
  4. View the new picture added by selecting it in the list of all pictures available.

Naming and location of the pictures

When the pictures are taken, they are not stored in SMS' database. They are stored under the *** folder.

The naming of the files goes as this:

INV: Prefix
_: Separator
Next 4: Store number
Next 4: Terminal number
_: Separator
Next 6: Transaction number (F1032)
_: Separator
Last 3: Image counter (001 to 999)

Examples:

Page 1: INV_00010901_000456_001.jpg
Page 2: INV_00010901_000456_002.jpg
Page 3: INV_00010901_000456_003.jpg
