eSuite Buy - Buying Processes

eSuite Buy > Buy

Information

The Buy entry in the eSuite Buy module is used for operations related to purchasing and receiving merchandise, billing and DSD (Direct Store Deliveries).

The buying process can be complete, passing from ordering and receiving the merchandise to the billing. In other cases, only certain operations, such as paying a bill or receiving the merchandise directly to the store may occur.

The following scenarios are possible:

  • Complete buying process
  • Receiving and billing
  • Billing
  • Direct store deliveries (DSD)

Complete buying process

The scenario below occurs in the complete buying process on backoffice workstations. It generally consists of ordering merchandise from a vendor, receiving the merchandise and paying the bill.

Order the merchandise

  1. Create a purchase order.
  2. Enter the items and quantities to order.
  3. Enter general information in the purchase order.
  4. Print the purchase order (under development).
  5. Close the purchase order.

Receive the merchandise

  1. Print the receiving(under development).
  2. Create a receiving document.
  3. Modify the quantities of the received items, if necessary.
  4. Add received items that were not ordered, if necessary,
  5. Print back orders, if necessary.
  6. Close the receiving.

Billing

  1. Create a billing document.
  2. Add or subtract billing data (matching the bill with the supplier's).
  3. Charge the bill to the account.
  4. Print the final bill (under development).
  5. Close the bill.

Receiving and billing

The scenario below occurs in recurrent orders, when the same merchandise is received periodically from the same vendor.

Receive the merchandise

  1. Create a receiving document.
  2. Enter general and shipping information.
  3. Enter the received items and quantities.
  4. Close the receiving.

Billing

  1. Create a billing document.
  2. Add or subtract billing data (matching the bill with the supplier's).
  3. Charge the bill to the account.
  4. Print the final bill (under development).
  5. Close the bill.

Billing

The scenario below occurs when checking the bill. The Billing operation is used to match your bill with the supplier's.

Billing

  1. Create a bill.
  2. Enter general information.
  3. Enter the received items and quantities.
  4. Add or subtract billing data (matching the bill with the supplier's).
  5. Charge the bill to your account.
  6. Close the bill.

DSD (Direct Store Deliveries)

The scenario below occurs when receiving merchandise directly to the store.

DSD operations include receiving and billing in one step.

  1. Create a DSD document.
  2. Enter general and shipping information.
  3. Enter the received items and quantities.
  4. Add fees and subtract billing data (matching the bill with the supplier's).
  5. Enter the bill payment or charge the bill to your account.
  6. Close the delivery.
