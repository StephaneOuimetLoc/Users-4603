Create Customers

eSuite > Customers > Master

Information

Customer accounts in eSuite may be created manually in the database.

Each account is identified by a code for which there are several rules that have to be observed:

  • The customer ID is a 14-character field. It is mandatory and unique, unless eSuite is configured for multi-stores (multi-targets). In this case, it is possible to have more than one customer with the same number, but with different targets.
  • Although the customer ID is a non-numeric field, remember that it must be entered at the points of sale. Some front-end workstations may not have alpha keypads.
  • The customer ID is not editable: once it is created, it cannot be modified.
  • The customer ID and information are automatically deployed to the front-end workstations once the new account information is saved.

Creating a customer account

Customer accounts are created with the Customer table. Follow the steps below.

In the Launcher, click the arrow in the Customers entry to open its menu and then:

  1. Click on Master.
  2. In the Start window, click on New: the New customer window opens, overlapping the work area.

  3. Enter the code for the new customer account.
  4. Click on OK: the Customer edit table opens on the desktop.

  5. Enter all the necessary information.
  6. Click on Save in the toolbar to save the changes.
