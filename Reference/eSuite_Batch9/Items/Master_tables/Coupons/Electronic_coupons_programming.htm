eSuite - Electronic Coupon Programming

eSuite > Items > Item auxiliaries

Information

Electronic coupons are store and vendor coupons given by the system when the specified criteria are reached. Like any item in the system, the electronic coupons have their own UPCs; they are considered negative items.

  • By default, the electronic store coupons are linked directly to the items and they affect the net sales.
  • By default, the electronic vendor coupons are added as new registrations in the transactions and they do not affect the net sales.

Electronic coupons may be programmed by entering their POS and price information and defining the criteria by means of the Electronic coupon link table.

However, eSuite offers the Coupon wizard that facilitates the creation of coupons by grouping all criteria (type, method, quantity, amount, etc.) that must be respected for the coupon to be applied.

For the electronic coupons to be issued, the Apply coupon links field of the Shopper level table must be checked in the corresponding shopper levels. The electronic coupons are given when the Subtotal key is pressed in the transactions.

By default, an electronic coupon is only given once in a transaction, even if its criteria are matched several times. Each item sold may participate in more than one electronic coupon, but only one electronic coupon discount may be applied on an item. However, some methods may be set in the electronic coupon definition to change the default behavior. For details, see the Coupons topic.

Electronic coupon example

The Coupon wizard is used to program the criteria that must be respected for the electronic coupons to be applied.

Below are the steps for programming a Buy X get Y free electronic coupon. For more examples, see Electronic coupons - examples.

Buy 3 bags of Oreo cookies, get 1 bag for free

There are two different ways of presenting this promotion:

  • The customer buys 3 bags of Oreo cookies and the free bag of Oreo cookies is taken among the 3 bags, or
  •  The customer must buy a 4th bag of Oreo cookies which will then be free.

The example below reflects the first presentation (the items for free are taken among the number of required items). The promotion is done for the bags of Oreo cookies bought in the same transaction.

 1. Add the electronic coupon criteria in the main section of the coupon wizard.
  Value field: select Buy X get Y free. 
  Buy X field: enter 3. 
  Get Y field: enter 1. 
  Coupon type field: select Electronic. 
  Description field: add a meaningful description. 
  Sub-department field: select the right sub-department. 
 
 
2. In the Criteria tab, click Add criteria. 
  Type field: select Item sales. 
  Equal to field: select the Oreo cookies item. 
  Minimum measure field: select Quantity. 
  Minimum value field: enter 3. 
  Select Make sure the discount affects the item(s) in these criteria.
This method (see Data view in Criteria tab) forces the system to apply the electronic coupon's discount on the ground beef item.
  Add a Manual description (optional).
  Click OK to confirm the changes. 
   
 
3. In the Details tab: 
  Funding field: select Store or Vendor.
  Event type field: select Sale. 
  Enter a Start date. 
  Enter an End date. 
   
 
4. In the Descriptions tab:
Expanded description: enter the expanded description of the promotion.  
   
 
 5. Click Save in the toolbar to finalize the coupon creation.
The system will automatically assign the record a coupon code. 
 
  The Criteria tab will display the following information (Text view and Data view):
      

In this example:

  • If the customer buys 2 bags of Oreo cookies, they will not get any item for free.
  • if the customer buys 3 bags of Oreo cookies, they will get 1 of the items for free.
  • If the customer buys 4 bags of Oreo cookies, they will get 1 of the items for free.
  • If the customer buys 6 bags of Oreo cookies in the same transaction, they will get 1 of the items for free, etc.

For the customer to get 2 bags of Oreo cookies for free when they buy 6 bags of Oreo cookies in the same transaction, add Based on validated criteria in the Multiplication and accumulation field (Add criteria window of the coupon wizard). This means that a bag of Oreo cookies will be given for free for every 3 bags of Oreo cookies bought by the customer in the same transaction.

NOTES:
In order to quantify the value of the electronic coupon, a price should be specified in the Price field (Price table) of each electronic coupon, even if its value is "items for free". This quantification defines the priority of the electronic coupon and is useful when a limited quantity of electronic coupons can be given. In this case, the best electronic coupon is given according to its classification (value set in the Price field multiplied by the number of times it can be given).
