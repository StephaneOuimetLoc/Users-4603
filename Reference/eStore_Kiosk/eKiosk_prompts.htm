eKiosk - Prompts

eKiosk

Information

The eKiosk contains some prompts here and there to request action from a customer. This page talks about the main prompts only.

Login

This prompt requires the customer to use his email or username and password to log into the kiosk. This allows him to view his account information, get points on item ordered and even apply coupons to his order.

Register

This prompt asks the customer to create a new loyalty account. The new account allows him to view his account information, get points on item ordered and even apply coupons to his order.
