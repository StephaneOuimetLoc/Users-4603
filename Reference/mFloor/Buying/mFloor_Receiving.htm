mFloor - Receiving

mFloor Procurement

Information

This document describes how to work with the receiving module and perform the tasks described below:

  • Receiving a closed purchase order or open receiving document
  • Creating a receiving document
  • Using the receiving application menu

Receiving a closed purchase order or open receiving document

The following procedure assumes that a purchase order has been created either in mFloor or in the Back office server or workstation, has been closed and submitted to the vendor, or that a receiving document has been created in mFloor or in the SMS Back office server or workstation and has an open status.

To receive a closed purchase order or open receiving document follow the procedure below:

  1. Tap or click on Receiving from the Buying module menu.
  2. The receiving menu opens on the Open receiving screen. The lower part of the screen (A) displays closed purchase orders ready to be received or open receiving documents. The difference between the two types of receiving documents is seen by looking for the PO# (B) Purchase orders that are closed and submitted to vendors display a PO#, open receiving documents do not show the PO#.

  3. Tap or click on the document to be received, the application displays the receiving header and information on the receiving document.

  4. There are two methods to retrieve the items in the receiving documents
    • Tap or click in the Enter item code box and scan the item to be received and go to Step 5.
      or

    • On the receiving application menu, in the upper right hand of the screen, tap or click List All Items.
    • The List line items screen appears displaying all the lines in the receiving document.
    • Tap or click on an item to bring up the details.
  6. Tap or click on the Case box to highlight the default quantity and either enter the amount to receive, or if the default value is correct, tap or click <ENTER> to accept the quantity.
  7. To receive the next item:
    • Tap or click the Enter item code and scan the next item.

      Or
    • Tap or click to return to the receiving document header.
    • On the receiving application menu, in the upper right hand of the screen, tap or click List Unscanned, this displays only those items that have not yet been received or scanned.
    • Choose the next item to receive and repeat steps 5 to 6c to cycle through all remaining items.
  9. When all lines have been received, on the receiving application menu click Close document to close off the receiving and update the inventory.

Creating a receiving document

The following procedure describes how to create a new receiving document using mFloor. It is similar to creating a purchase order document in that items must be linked to a vendor through the cost table in order for the items to display under a specific vendor.

To create a receiving document follow the procedure below:

  1. On the Receiving screen tap or click
  2. At the vendor list tap or click the desired vendor.
  3. To add items to the receiving document:
    • Tap or click the Enter item code and scan the first item.
      or

    • Tap or click to open a list of items available through this vendor.
    • Tap or click an item.
    • Tap or click the Case box, enter the quantity and tap or click <ENTER>.
  5. To add other items to the receiving document repeat steps 3a to 3d.
  6. When all items have been added to the receiving document tap or click to return to receiving document header.
  7. On the receiving application menu tap or click Close document to close off receiving document and update inventory.

Using the receiving application menu

When a user is in a receiving document an icon appears for the receiving application menu in the top right hand corner of the screen.  

To perform the actions described below on a receiving document tap or a click on and then tap or click the associated menu action.

  • To show all items in the current receiving document regardless of their status tap or click List All Items.
  • To show only those items that have not yet been received tap or click List Unscanned.
  • To show those items for which there are short shipped tap or click List Shorted.
  • To go back to the receiving document header tap or click Document Header.
  • To print the receiving document to the default printer tap or click Print report.
  • To void the receiving document tap or click Mark as void.
  • To suspend the receiving document tap or click Suspend document.
  • To close the receiving document tap or click Close document.
