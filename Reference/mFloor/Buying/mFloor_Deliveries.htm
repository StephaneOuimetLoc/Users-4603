mFloor - Deliveries (DSD) Direct Store Deliveries

mFloor Procurement

Information

This document describes how to work with the Deliveries module and perform the following tasks:

  • Creating and adding items to a new delivery order
  • Opening a previously suspended delivery order document
  • Preparing a delivery for invoice total
  • Using the main delivery order application menu

Creating and adding items to a new delivery (DSD)

To create and add items to a delivery the items must be associated to a vendor through the cost table, which must be done in SMS. If the items to add to the delivery are already associated with a vendor, proceed below to creating a new delivery.

To create a new delivery on a vendor follow the steps below:

  1. The Direct Store Deliveries menu opens to the Open DSD screen. If any open DSD already exist they are displayed in section (A).
  2. To add a new purchase order tap or click in the upper right had corner.
  3. The new delivery screen appears displaying a list of vendors (B). To scroll through the list of vendors swipe the screen or if using a mouse, click and drag on the list.
  4. Tap or click a vendor name to choose it.
  5. Once a vendor is chosen the delivery document header appears displaying the vendor name, transaction number, and associated dates.
  6. To add items to the new delivery:
    1. Type the item code in the Enter item code box at the top of the screen.

      or
    2. Tap or click and scan the item code using the camera. The scanner from a mobile device can also be used.

      or
    3. Tap or click and a list of items for the vendor appears and choose an item by tapping or clicking it. In the case of a long list swipe or click and drag to scroll the list.
    4. At the item screen enter the quantity in the Case box.
    5. Scan the next item or Enter the code in Enter item code or click to go back and choose another item from Quick search.
    6. Repeat all of the above steps (a-e) to add items to the delivery.

Opening a previously suspended delivery document

When working with delivery documents, users can suspend documents using the application menu or documents are automatically suspended when users tap or click when leaving a delivery document.

To open a previously suspended delivery document follow the steps below:

  1. If not already in the mFloor application, open the mFloor application and log in.
  2. At the main menu of the mFloor application tap or click Buying module.
  3. At the Buying module main menu click Deliveries
  4. The Open DSD menu appears and if open deliveries exist will display in section (A), if the list is long, swipe or click and scroll to view all orders.
  5. Tap or click on the desired delivery order to open it.

Preparing a delivery for invoice total and recording a payment

When a delivery document is completed and all items have been scanned users can close the document or prepare the delivery for an invoice total.

To prepare a delivery for invoice total follow the steps below:

  1.  If already in a delivery document, at the end of the last item scanned click to return to the Delivery header screen or open an existing delivery document.
  2. On Delivery application menu click Invoice Total
  3. The Payment mode window opens to display the vendor, total cases ordered, total purchase amount and outstanding balance.
  4. To make a payment on a delivery click Application menu and click one of the modes of payment as shown below.
    • Drop Payment
    • Charge to Account
    • Payment by Cash
    • Payment by Check
    In each case, when a payment method is chosen a small window prompts the user for the amount to pay with that payment menthod.

    NOTE: If requried different payment methods can be used for the same delivery document. In this case the Invoice Total window displays the breakdown of the various payments.

Using the main delivery order application menu

When a user is in a delivery document an icon appears for the delivery application menu in the top right hand corner of the screen.  

To perform the actions described below on a purchase order document tap or a click on and then tap or click the associated menu action.

  • To show items in the current delivery tap or click List line items.
  • To show the invoice header of the current delivery tap or click Invoice Header.
  • To print the delivery report to the default printer tap or click Print report.
  • To void the current delivery tap or click Mark as void.
  • To suspend the current delivery tap or click Suspend document.
  • To view the payment screen for the current delivery tap or click Invoice Total.
