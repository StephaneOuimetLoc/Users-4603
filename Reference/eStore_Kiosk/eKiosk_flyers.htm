eKiosk - Flyers

eKiosk

Information

The Flyers page of the eKiosk allows the customer to view the current store flyer live and see product's information directly by selecting an item on the flyer.

From this page, when selecting an item, it is possible to add it to your personal wish list and add it to your order. See below what the prompt looks like.

How to manage the Flyers

To manage the Flyers, please refer to the LOC SMS eKiosk - Flyer Management in SMSMembers.chm.
