eSuite Care - KPI Reports

eSuite Care > KPI

Information

The KPI (Key performance indicator) reports allow measuring department performance, comparing sales, displaying customer visits and item quantities sold by customer type. They also allow identifying customers that have been inactive for a certain period, who the most loyal customers are for a given store and where they actively shop.

The KPI reports are available from eSuite Care:

  1. In the launcher, click on KPI.
  2. Select the right type of report from the Start window.
 

KPI reports:

  • Customer mix
  • Customer turnover
  • Department impact
  • Totalizer impact
  • Inactivity
  • Loyalty

Customer mix

The Customer mix wizard offers criteria selection that includes period, date range and target. It allows breaking down the information by date, store (default), operator and department. The More criteria button adds criteria related to operators and departments.

The Customer mix report displays the sales and allows comparison by customer type:

  • Unregistered customers hold only an SMSPayment loyalty card and no customer account record in the eSuite customer tables.
    The card number cannot be linked to a person and the store has no way of communicating with the customer.
  • Registered customers hold a customer record in eSuite customer tables and can be identified.
    The store has the necessary information to identify the customer as a person and may contact them by phone or by email, depending on the information in the customer record.
  • Loyalty members represent the totality of registered and unregistered customers.
    It allows comparing the difference in sales between loyalty customers as a whole and the walk-in customers.
  • Walk-in customers have no store identification number and cannot be identified by the store.

The Customer mix report includes amounts spent, number of visits and quantity or volume of items purchased.

In the bottom menu:

  • Analyze breaks down the report information by date, store, operator and department.
    It provides access to queries for further drill-down to previous results in customer lists.

Customer turnover

The Customer turnover wizard offers criteria selection that includes period, date range and target. It allows breaking down the information by store and department. The More criteria button adds criteria related to dates.

The Customer turnover report displays the customer movement among the different customer statuses from three periods (the current period and the two previous ones). It also shows sales by members of the store and non-members. All customers hold a customer record in the eSuite customer tables and in SMSPayment.

In the bottom menu:

  • Analyze breaks down the report information by store and department.
    It provides access to queries for further drill-down to previous results in customer lists.

Below is a brief description of the information displayed by default in the Customer turnover report.

COLUMN DESCRIPTION
Store ID The store number.
Customer
Start count The number of customers who made purchases in the second previous period based on the period preceding the current.
New count The number of customers who have made purchases in the current period and made no purchases in any of the two previous periods.
Lost count The number of customers who have made no purchases in the current period and made purchases in the first and/or second period.
Back count The number of customers who have made purchases in the current period and in the first previous period, and made no purchase in the second previous period.
Variation The difference between the lost count customers and the total of new count and back count customers.
End count The difference between the customer start count and the customer variation.
Amount
Total The total amount of sales for the store members and the non members.
Member The amount of sales for the store members.
Non member The amount of sales for the non members.
Member vs. total The percentage that the difference between the sales of non members represent compared to the total sales.

NOTE: This report requires additional configurations to function. See your reseller for details.

Loyalty

The Loyalty wizard offers criteria selection that includes period, date range and target. It allows breaking down the information by date and store.

 

The Loyalty report displays the sales by customer type to determine which store the customer account belongs to, based on the majority of sales during the past three months.

  • Unregistered customers hold only a SMSPayment loyalty card and no customer account record in the eSuite customer tables.
    The card number cannot be linked to a person and the store has no way of communicating with the customer.
  • Active customers hold an account record in the eSuite customer tables. 
    Based on the first transaction, a store number is defined in the Calling field (Store section) of the Customer table. This may change depending on the majority of the sales during the previous three months.
  • Created customers hold an account record in the eSuite customer tables.
    Based on the first transaction, a store number is defined for in the Created field (Store section) of the Customer table.
  • Guest customers hold an account record in the eSuite customer tables.
    Based on where they are currently considered active, a store number is defined in the Calling field (Store section) of the Customer table..
    They are considered guest customerswhen they make a purchase in a different store than the active one.

In the bottom menu:

  • Analyze displays the report information by date and store.
    It provides access to queries for further drill-down to previous results in customer lists.
