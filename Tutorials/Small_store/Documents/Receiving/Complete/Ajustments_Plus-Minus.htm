Adjustments Plus and Minus

You can add adjustments to a receiving using the Complete functions:

From the Context Tools, click on the hamburger icon  below the current transaction and select "Complete functions".
 

From the Complete window, you can enter the adjustments in order to add or substract from the receiving depending on the function.
 

To enter an amount. Double-click on the box under the Entry column for the required function and add the value.
