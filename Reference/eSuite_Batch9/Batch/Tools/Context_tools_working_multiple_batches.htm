Working With Multiple Batches                       

eSuite > Items > Batches

Information

The eSuite software allows working with multiple batches at the same time and on the same desktop.

Batch-related actions are performed from the Batches entry in the Launcher or through Context Tools. The present document focuses on how to work with multiple batches using the Context Tools window.

Displaying multiple batches on the same desktop

The scenario below describes how to use Context Tools to open two price-related batches at the same time and on the same desktop.

In the Launcher, click the arrow in the Items entry to display its menu and then:

  1. Click on Batches: the list of batch-related entries opens in the Start window.
  2. In Context Tools, click on Select a batch in Current batches: the Batch window opens on the desktop.
    • The Search field will look up batch names.
    • The Macro pull-down menu will drill down the search to the type.
  3. Select All price related from the Macro field menu.
  4. Click Go: the list of the existing price batches opens.
  5. Click on two batches while pressing and holding down the Ctrl key:
  6. Click OK: the selected batches are listed in the Current batches section of Context Tools.

  7. Click Open list  on the right of the Price batch. This will open the Price batch wizard:
  8. Click Open list  on the right of the Price sale batch. This will open the Price sale batch wizard:
  9. In the Price batch wizard, check the Show only items into the batch box and click Execute. This will open the list of items that are part of the price batch.
  10. In the Price sale batch wizard, check the Show only items into the batch box and click Execute. This will open the list of items that are part of the price sale batch.

NOTES:
 Working with multiple batches at the same time allows to easily edit, add items or switch in and out of each batch at any moment.
