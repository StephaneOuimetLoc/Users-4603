Table of content

Information

The Risk level table keeps credit limit (store charge) or check cashing information, and may be used to group customers by these common settings. Each record of the Risk level table is programmed with a credit limit, used as a credit profiler for each customer.

From the launcher in eSuite Care, Manage, Buy, Sell or Batch, click the down arrow in the Customer enter to display the Customer menu.

Click on Customer auxiliaries. The Start windows opens up for the customer auxiliaries.

Creating a Risk level

  1. Click on the Risk Levels. A risk levels list grid will display on screen
  2. Click on the + sign to add a risk level
  3. Enter Risk level
  4. Enter appropriate Descriptor, example: check $200 / charge $100 limit (minimum recommended requirements)
  5. Enter Credit limit amount
  6. Enter values in the fields to match description accordingly.
  7. Enter values for check
  8. Click on the Private card tab and enter the private card information
  9. Click on the checkmark to save

NOTE: The risk level 1 must exist in the database since it is the basic risk level. i.e. if no customer is entered for a transaction, the risk level 1 is used by default.
