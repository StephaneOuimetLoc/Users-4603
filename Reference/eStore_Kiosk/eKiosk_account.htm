eKiosk - Account Tab

eKiosk

Information

The Account page of the eKiosk allows the customer to view/edit his profile information.

Account Features

This section of the eKiosk contains certain features to help the customer to view and update his account information, preferences and activity, this page supports:

  • Contact Information:The customer can edit his contact information like First Name, Last Name, Email, etc.

  • Addresses: Allows the customer change billing and delivery addresses.

  • Preferences:Allows the customer to modify specific preferences Food preferences, Communication preferences, Delivery preferences.

  • Cards:Allows the customer to manage loyalty or club cards.

  • Points and Clubs:Allows the customer to manage points programs and clubs.

The Kiosk provides the virtual popup keyboard for facilitating the entering of information in all text fields.
