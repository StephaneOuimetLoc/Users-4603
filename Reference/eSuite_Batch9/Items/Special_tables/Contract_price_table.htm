eSuite - Contract Price Table

eSuite > Items > Master > Item list

Information

The Contract price table contains particular prices on items for specific customers. Each customer may have a particular price for the same item.

Displaying the Contract price table

In the Launcher, click the arrow in the Items entry to display its menu and then:

  1. Click on Master: the Start window opens in the Items work area.
  2. Click on Item list: the Wizard tab opens in the same window, displaying the Items - General List.
  3. Enter the search criteria and click Execute: results will display in a record grid, in the Items tab of the same window.
  4. Select a record and click on Edit on the bottom of the item grid: the option menu is displayed.
  5. Select Special items and then Contract price: the Contract price edit table opens overlapping the work area.

Layout

The Contract price table is divided in two parts: the main section and the Contract price table grid.

Main section

* Click on a field to get information on it.

Contract price table grid

* Click on a field to get information on it.

The Contract price table grid offers quick information on particular item prices for specific customers. The following columns are displayed: Customer and Price.

Columns are movable, resizable and may be selected/deselected for a customized experience. They allow sorting the records in an ascending or a descending order.
Records are displayed in ascending order by customer identifier. Selecting a record allows displaying and editing its information in the main section of the Contract price table.

The Round field

The Round field contains the ending number of the particular price. It works in combination with the Margin field.

Two different ending numbers may be specified in the Round field, separated by a comma. In this case, any price that ends with a five (5) or less will end with the first ending number, and any price that ends above a five (5) will end with the second ending number.

Back to layout

The Price field

The Price field contains the particular price of the item for the customer.

The Price field is split in two fields:

  1. The Price amount that contains the particular price of the item for the customer.
  2. The Price quantity that contains the quantity of the item that may be purchased at the particular price.

The point of sale will sell the item at the particular price to that customer up to and including the quantity specified. Then, if the quantity purchased by the customer exceeds the quantity specified, the rest of the items will be sold at the retail price defined in the Price table.

The Suggested price (round) field

The Suggested price (round) contains the ending number of the particular suggested price. It works in combination with the Suggested price (margin) field.

Two (2) different ending numbers may be specified in the Suggested price (round) field, separated by a comma. In this case, any price that ends with a five (5) or less will end with the first ending number, and any price that ends above a five (5) will end with the second ending number.

The Suggested price (round) field is used for information purposes only, and may appear in some reports.
