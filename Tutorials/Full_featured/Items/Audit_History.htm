Audit History Report - Overview

eSuite > Items > Master> Audit History 

Information

This document describes the concept of Audit History under the Items section of eSuite Manage/Gas/Care/Buy or Gas.

Concept

The Audit History is a report showing all the changes done to the records in the Item Master tables.

Key Points:

  • Shows when any field has been modified and displays the olf value and new value of that field.
  • Displays changes to any of the item master tables (OBJ_TAB, POS_TAB, PRICE_TAB, COST_TAB, ECL_TAB, BMP_TAB, LOC_REV, CLS_TAB)
  • Available in Single Store, Multi-Target as well as Multi-Tenant.

Access Audit History

To access the Audit History click on the Items section in  the Manage module. Then select Audit History

Note: Purge: The Audit report depends on how much data is being retained in the revision tables (i.e PRICE_REV).The revision tables are purged on a regular basis.You can find out how much data is being kept in the revision tables by asking your dealer to check the purge settings for REVISION in the setup console in the SYS module. The default setting is 14, so 14 days of data will be kept and you wil be able to consult the report 14 days back.
