mFloor - Manager Module

mFloor Manage

Information

The mFloor Manager module, accessible from the Main menu, is used for balancing functions such as loans, pickups and declares and provides a manager a convenient method of performing these transactions on a mobile device while the cashier continues to serve customers at the POS.

Accessing the Manager module

To access the Manager module and the various drawer functions, follow these steps:

  1. Click or tap Manager module from the mFloor Main menu.
  2. On the Manager module main screen click or tap Drawer.

Performing Balancing transactions

The Drawer module contains the main functions for working with drawers including:

  • Making loans to operators
  • Performing pickups from operators
  • Declaring operators

The module opens to a  main screen called Operator summary and displays each operator that have amounts in their drawer.

NOTE: The Operator summary screen may be empty if no operators have been given loans or have pickups.

To view all operators regardless if they have amounts in their drawer, click or press on the application button and click or tap Show all Operators.

The application menu is also used to access other views

  • Refresh Summary: Updates the Operator summary view to show the most recent transactions.
  • Show All Operators: Displays all operators on the Operator summary screen regardless of their drawer status.
  • Show Big Short/Over: Displays operators in the Operator summary who are short or over by more than $5.

Loaning money to an operator drawer

  1. At the Operator summary click or tap the application menu and click or tap Show All Operators.
  2. Click or tap an operator to display the Drawer transaction menu.
  3. Click or tap Loan and on the Loan screen click or tap the Cash entry.
  4. At the Loan Cash prompt change the amount and click or tap OK to accept the amount.
  5. Click the application menu and click CClose Transaction to complete the loan.

Performing an operator drawer Pickup

  1. At the Operator summary click or tap the application menu and click or tap Show All Operators.
  2. Click or tap an operator to display the Drawer transaction menu.
  3. Click or tap Pickup and on the Pickup screen for the operator click or tap the line of the tender to be picked up.
  4. At the Pickup prompt change the amount and click or tap OK to accept the amount.
  5. Click the application menu and click Close Transaction to complete the pickup.

Declaring an operator drawer

  1. Click or tap on the operator and click or tap Declare.
  2. At the Declare prompt type "1" for Yes and 0 for No.
  3. At the the Declare tender prompt enter the declared amount and click or tap OK. 
  4. Click or tap the application menu and then click or tap Close transaction to finish the Declare.
NOTE: If an error is made and needs to be corrected, do another declare after the correction. The last declare of the day is the valid one.
