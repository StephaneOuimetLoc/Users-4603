eSuite - Split Code Example

eSuite > Items > Master > Item list

Information

Split codes allow to purchase items in one format and sell them in two different formats. For example, the store can purchase cases of Pepsi and sell both cases and single cans or bottles.

Item inquiries will show the movement in both formats. However, the total quantity on hand is kept in the single unit of measure.

NOTE: Before setting up split codes, any inventory quantities showing on the largest unit of measure MUST be transferred to the smallest unit of measure.
