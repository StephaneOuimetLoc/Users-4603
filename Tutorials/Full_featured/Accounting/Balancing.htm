Operator & Safe Balancing with LOC SMS

Cashier and Safe balancing numbers are reported in multiple LOC SMS reports. This document sheds light on these reports.

All totals in LOC SMS are available from the Store Multi Totals report. The balancing figures are split into multiple sections that this document will explain.

In order to visualize what each section is for, let us first go through a regular operation and concentrate on the operator section. Clients are balancing their operators either daily or weekly. This makes the operator balancing much easier because the loop is closed in a day or a week. The steps are: loaning money at the beginning; selling items; doing pickups; declaring the operators at the end. Operators are starting with 0 responsibility and will finish their shifts with 0 responsibility.

In comparison, the safe is more complex because the amounts are accumulated and calculated, so we need to keep track of opening and closing totals for a safe during a balancing period (daily or weekly).

Terminal Balancing

In LOC SMS, it is possible to do terminal or operator balancing. The terminal balancing requires a configuration in the System.ini file (Terminal prefix parameter under the Customer part).

When doing terminal balancing, it is important to know that all totalizers related to the balancing of the drawer will be reported under the virtual operator created for the terminal balancing. All other totalizers will be reported under the "physical" operator.

Safe Balancing

By default, LOC SMS keeps figure totals by day, by week, by month and by year. Depending on the period you choose, the reports will add all amounts by date. Earlier dates can also be selected. The Operator balancing report or the Multi totals by operator report will show the same figures.

Safe reports are more challenging because they need to keep a running total day after day, week after week. You need to be careful in your report selection since the weekly numbers are not necessarily the addition of the daily numbers, the Store Multi Totals report will not give the same result if you select daily, weekly for the actual week or weekly for the previous week.

However, the reports available in the Accounting section of the Store reports (SMS Pro/Reports/Financial/Store/Accounting) will always give what you are expecting as your safe balancing figures. The Store Multi Totals report will also give the safe balancing figures, but you need to filter what you are looking for. The safe ending figure in the Store Multi Totals report is where there will be a variance, so stay with the Safe reports.

Troubleshooting tips

Here are some tips to help troubleshooting issues.

The tender to pickup is not visible in the tender list

Verify in the Function table that the manager or the user has sufficient permissions for that tender.
