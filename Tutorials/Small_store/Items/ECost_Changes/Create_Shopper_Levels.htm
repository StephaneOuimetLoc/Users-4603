Information

The Shopper level table contains special pricing information for customers, sometimes referred to as the Preferred shopper level. It is used to group customer for particular promotions. Each record in the Shopper level table must be linked to a price level to establish the item prices used for the group of customers.

NOTE: The shopper level 1 is the default used for regular customers when no customer is entered in the transaction.

From the launcher in eSuite Care, Manage, Buy, Sell or Batch, click the down arrow in the Customer enter to display the Customer menu.

Click on Customer auxiliaries. The Start window opens up for the customer auxiliaries.

Creating a Shopper level                                                                 

  1.          Click on Shopper Levels. A shopper level list will display on screen. 
                                            
  2. Click on the + sign to add a shopper level
  3. Enter Shopper level
  4. Enter appropriate Descriptor
  5. Select Price level, example: Preferred Customer, Price level 5 (minimum recommended requirements)
  6. Enter promotional values you would like to pass on to the customers assigned to this level     
  7. Enter loyalty values you would like to pass on to the customer assigned to this level including points program, methods, expired days, etc.
  8. Click on the Discount tab and enter the discount information
  9. Click on the Upgrade tab and enter the upgrade information     
  10. Click on the checkmark to save

       

To modify a Shopper Level

WARNING: Modifications to the Shopper Level table will affect all customers assigned to that level

  1. From the grid list, select / highlight the level to modify
  2. Double click OR press F3 on your keyboard OR select Edit from the bottom grid menu
  3. Make the revision desired
  4. Save
