Table of contents

Information

The cash tender is used when the customers pay using Cash.

In order to use Cash as the method of payment, you will need to do the following steps:

  1. Log into ePos as a cashier or manager
  2. Run a/some transaction(s).
  3. Click on Sub-total
  4. Select cash as the method of payment. It will display a message saying "Cash?" on the entry window.
  5. Enter the amount that the customer has given to you. Complete the transaction

You can select the cash button instead of clicking Subtotal > tender.


You also have the option to select between $5.00, $10.00 or $20.00 cash amount instead of typing in the actual amount.
