eSuite - Future Instore Price Table

eSuite > Items > Batches > Price Information

Information

The Future instore table is used to change the future instore prices of the selected items. It contains the future instore price information.

Displaying the Future instore price table

In the Launcher, click the arrow in the Items entry to display its menu and then:

  1. Click on Batches: the Start window opens in the Batches work area.
  2. Click on Price information: the Price batch wizard opens in a new tab.
  3. In the Batch type field, select Instore.
  4. In the Batch field, select an instore batch.
  5. Enter all other necessary criteria.
  6. Press Execute: results will display in the Price instore batch grid.
  7. Select a record and click Edit in the bottom menu of the grid.
  8. Select Price batch, then Instore: the Future instore prices table opens over the work area.

Layout

The Future instore price table is divided in two parts: the main section and the Future instore price table grid.

Content

Here is the description of the Future instore price table fields and their functionalities:

FIELD DESCRIPTION
Main section
Batch The Batch field contains the batch identifier.
It is not editable. Its value comes from the Batch ID of the Batch header table and is automatically displayed when a future instore record is added, linking the future instore record to the current batch.
Target The Target field contains the target identifier (the store group).
When a future instore record is added, the target value of the current price record is automatically displayed in the Target field.
It is grayed out (not editable) unless the Multi target option in the System.ini file is selected.
Level The Level field contains the price level number.
When a future instore record is added, the level value of the current price record is automatically displayed in the Level field.
Start date The Start date field contains the starting date of the future instore event, i.e. the date on which the future instore event becomes effective. The starting date is inclusive.
When a future instore record is added, the start date of the Batch header table is automatically displayed in the Start date field. The starting date will become the instore starting date in the corresponding price record.
End date The End date field contains the ending date of the future instore events, i.e. the date on which the future instore event is no longer effective. The ending date is inclusive.
When a future instore record is added, the ending date of the Batch header table is automatically displayed in the End date field. The ending date will become the instore ending date of the corresponding price record.
Price The Price field contains the future instore price of the item.
When a future instore record is added, the current instore price of the item is automatically displayed in the Price field.
Quantity (Price) The Quantity (Price) field contains the instore quantity for the price.
It is automatically displayed when the future instore record is added.
TGM (Price) The TGM Price field contains the future instore profit margin on the item, in percentage.
It is used to generate the item instore price.
Package price The Package price field contains the future instore package price of the item, i.e. a special price if a certain quantity of the item is bought by a customer within the same transaction.
When a future instore record is added, the current instore package price of the item is automatically displayed in the Package price field.
The Apply package price field must be checked in the corresponding shopper levels for the Package price to work.
See the Package field for more information on the package price behavior.
Quantity (Package price) The Quantity (Package price) field contains the instore quantity for the package price.
TGM (Package price) The TGM (Package price) field contains the future instore package price profit margin on the item, in percentage.
It is used to generate the item instore package price.
Limited The Limited field contains the instore price for which a customer may purchase the limited quantity of the item.
Quantity (Limited) The Quantity (Limited) field limits the quantity of an item a customer may purchase at a special instore price.
When a future instore record is added, the current instore limited quantity of the item is automatically displayed in the Quantity (Limited) field.
TGM (Limited) The TGM (Limited) field contains the future instore limited quantity profit margin on the item, in percentage.
It is used to generate the item instore limited quantity price.
Priority TPR The Priority TPR field specifies whether the future instore price overrides the TPR price (checked), or not (unchecked).
Priority Sale The Priority Sale field specifies whether the future instore price overrides all prices (checked), or not (unchecked).
Discount $ The Discount $ (Dsc $) field contains the future amount of discount applied on the item when it is sold at the instore price.
When a future instore record is added, the current instore amount of discount applied on the item is automatically displayed in the Discount $ field.
The Apply item disc $ field must be checked in the corresponding shopper levels for the Discount $ to work.
Discount % The Discount % (Dsc %) field contains the future percentage of discount applied on the item when it is sold at the instore price.
When a future instore record is added, the current instore percentage of discount applied on the item is automatically displayed in the Discount % field.
The Apply item disc % field must be checked in the corresponding shopper levels for the Discount % to work.
Points The Points field contains the future number of points given to the customer when he buys the item at the instore price.
When a future instore record is added, the current number of points given at the instore price for the item is automatically displayed in the Points field.
The Preset items point method must be selected in the Point method field of the corresponding shopper levels for the points to be given according to the item definition.
Mix The Mix field links the item to a mix & match, which is used to mix items to match them to an instore price.
When a future instore record is added, the current instore mix & match value of the item is automatically displayed in the Mix field.
Method The Method field contains the identification letters of the future instore price method used, corresponding to a special instore price behavior.
When a future instore record is added, the current instore price method of the item is automatically displayed in the Method field.
