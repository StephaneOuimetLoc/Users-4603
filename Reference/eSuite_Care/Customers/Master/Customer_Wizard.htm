You may or may not have noticed that there are new options in the "More criteria" as well as the new alphabetical order in which it is now displayed.

This makes it easier to read and located the listed options you are searching for criteria's you wish to add to your search query.

In order to facilitate Customer search by name we have added 2 new search fields - Customer first name and Customer last name. These will make it easier to find who you are looking for as some end-user have changed the automatic populated "Legal name" field to the customer's company name of said Customer's (Last,First names) details. Which at times would make it difficlut to search by the actual Customer name.

Once you have selected on or both of these fields you may then select "OK" button and they will be apart of your query after you enter the text that you are seaching for.

Simply click on the Execute button and the system will bring up all customers who may have the vaule you have entered.

*note: In this example the query only found 1 result.
