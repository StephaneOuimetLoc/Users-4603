eSuite Manage - Hourly Reports

eSuite Manage > Dimensions > Hourly

Information

The hourly reports produce sales figures to balance the main financial information by hour, for the period and dates requested. They are available from the eSuite Manage module.

Access:

  1. In the launcher, click on Dimensions.
  2. Click on Hourly: the Start window opens, displaying the types of hourly reports.
  3. Select the right type of report.

Hourly reports:

  • Single total
  • Multi totals
  • Compare totals

Single total

The Single total wizard offers criteria selection that includes period, date range, totalizer and target. It allows breaking down the information by date and by store. Criteria related to movement may be added with the Add criteria button.

The Single total report displays one total by hour for the selected totalizers and period.

In the bottom menu:

  • Analyze allows displaying the report information by date and by store.

Multi totals

The Multi totals wizard offers criteria selection that includes period, date range, target and totalizer range. It allows breaking down the information by date and by store. The Use multi selection checkbox allows selecting multiple totalizers.

Criteria related to movement may be added with the Add criteria button.

The Multi Totals report displays all totals by hour for the selected period and totalizers.

In the bottom menu:

  • Analyze allows displaying the report information by date and by store.
    It also provides access to the single total and the compare totals reports.

Back to top

Compare totals

The Compare totals wizard offers criteria selection that includes period, reference date and compare date, reference target and compare target, reference totalizer and compare totalizer. It allows breaking down the report information by date and by store. Criteria related to movement may be added with the Add criteria button.

The Compare totals report displays hourly information related to quantity, amount and weight from a store compared to another and from a totalizer compared to another.

In the bottom menu:

  • Analyze allows displaying the report information by date and by store.
