Pay with Foreign Currencies

ePos >Functions> Tenders > Foreign currencies

Table of contents

Information

The Foreign currency tender is used when the customers pay using foreign currency. The exchange rate is defined in the foreign currency function.

In order to use foreign currency as the method of payment, you will need to do the following steps:

  1. Log into ePos as a cashier or manager
  2. Run a/some transaction(s).
  3. Click on Sub-total
  4. Select foreign as the method of payment. It will display a message saying "Foreign currency?" on the entry window.
  5. Enter the amount that the customer has given to you. Complete the transaction
