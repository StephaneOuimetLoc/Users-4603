eSuite - Gadget Reports

eSuite Gas > Gadgets

Information

GadgetXXXXXXXXXX reports give an overview of multiple information such as sales, sales by grade, sales by type, sales by mode, sales by exception and balancing for the store. Report can be execute from Store  and from the Host.

To access the gadget reports:

  1. In the launcher, click on Gas
  2. Click on Gadgets: the Start window opens, displaying the gadget-related options
  3. Select the right type of report
Gadget reports:
  • Sales "Check tables"
  • Sales by grade
  • Sales by type
  • Sales by mode
  • Sales by exception
  • XBalancing window

Sales "Check tables"

The Sales "Check tables" offers criteria selection that includes period, date range and target. It allows breaking down the information by date, by store.

The Sales "Check Tables" report displays amounts and volume.

In the bottom menu:

  • Analyze allows breaking down information by store, by date or all transactions, displaying transaction analysis and performing subqueries and linked queries.

Sales by grade

The Sales by grade wizard offers criteria selection that includes period, date range and target. It allows breaking down the information by date and by store.

The Sales by grade report displays details for all gas sub-departments for amount and volume.

In the bottom menu:

  • Analyze allows breaking down information by store or by date, displaying pump analysis and performing subqueries and linked queries.
