eSuite Manage - Gift Card Reports

eSuite Manage > Balancing > Gift

Information

The Gift card reports produce information related to the gift card transactions that can be performed with eSuite.

They are available from eSuite Manage:

  1. In the launcher, click on Balancing
  2. Click on Gift: the Start window opens, displaying the options related to gift cards
  3. Select the right type of report

Gift card reports:

  • Balance
  • Adjustments
  • Single total
  • Multi totals
  • Transaction details
  • Statement
  • Activity summary
  • Liability
  • Compensation
  • Matching (future development)

Single total

The Single total report wizard offers criteria selection that includes period range, totalizer and target. It allows breaking down the information by date, by store and by gift card. Criteria related to the gift card may be added with the Add criteria button.

The Single total report displays one gift card total according to the selected criteria.

In the bottom menu:

  • Analyze allows performing Customer and Gift card subqueries and linked queries.
    It provides access to details on the gift card transactions.

Multi totals

The Multi totals wizard offers criteria selection that includes period range and target. It allows breaking down the information by date, by store and by gift card. Criteria related to the gift card may be added with the Add criteria button.

The Multi totals report displays all gift card totals for the selected period range and target.

In the bottom menu:

  • Analyze allows performing Customer and Gift card subqueries and linked queries.
    It provides access to details on the gift card transactions.

Back to top

Detailed transactions

The Detailed transactions wizard offers criteria selection related to period range, target and authorization number. It allows including status changes. Additional criteria may be added with the Add criteria button.

The Detailed transactions report displays details (store ID, date, gift card number, transaction, totalizer, etc.) of all gift card transactions for the selected criteria.

In the bottom menu, the Analyze button provides three options:

  • All displays the details of the selected transaction and the summary of its total amounts by totalizer.

  • The Detail option displays the information of the transaction in detail:


    Clicking on + displays the keystrokes used in the transaction:

Gift card statement

The Gift card account statement will display the account gift card balance and activity by date and grouped by account number. It allows breaking down by date, by customer ID, gift card number and target. Additional criteria may be added with the Add criteria button.

NOTE: You can use comma separated search for customer id (ex.: 2, 5, 7) or gift card (ex.:1002, 3000, 3200). This method only populates the items listed explicitly even if others exist in that range.

Activity summary

The Activity summary wizard offers criteria selection related to period range and target. It allows breaking down information by date, by store and by gift card and showing gift cards with no movement only. Additional criteria may be added with the Add criteria button.

The Activity summary report displays the summary of the gift card movement for all gift cards, according to the selected criteria.

In the bottom menu:

  • Edit allows editing the information in the selected column.
    It provides access to the following tables: Customer, Alternate codes, Shopper level, Risk level and Customer group.
  • Analyze allows performing Customer and Gift card subqueries and linked queries.
    It provides access to transaction analysis.

Back to top

Liability

The Liability wizard offers criteria selection related to period, date and balance. It allows breaking down information by gift card. Additional criteria may be added with the Add criteria button.

The Liability report displays the balance on the gift cards displayed in the report according to the criteria in the Liability wizard.

In the bottom menu:

  • Analyze allows performing Customer and Gift card subqueries and linked queries.
    It provides access to transaction analysis.

 

Back to top

Account statement

The Account statement wizard offers criteria selection related to period range, customer account range and customer group range. It allows breaking down information by gift card. Additional criteria may be added with the Add criteria button.

The Account statement report displays information related to gift cards according to the customer accounts and groups selected in the wizard.
