mFloor - Inventory Filters

mFloor Procurement

Information

The mFloor Inventory module provides two filters to help users narrow down searching for items when creating and adding items to inventory counts. The two available filters on the inventory module main screen are Filter by vendor and Filter by sub-dept.

Applying Filters in mFloor Inventory module

  • Both filters work in the same way and bring up a list of vendors or sub-depts from which the user can choose.
  • Both filters can be applied at the same time and will affect the next New inventory document and remain applied to that document until it is closed.

To apply filters by vendor or sub-department, follow this procedure:

  1. If not already in mFloor, log in and click or tap Inventory module.
  2. At the Inventory main menu click or tap either Filter by vendor or Filter by sub-dept
  3. In each case the filter displays the appropriate list.
  4. Click or tap the desired vendor or sub-department depending on the filter.
    Filter by vendor
    Filter by sub-department
  5. A confirmation message appears, click or tap OK to close confirmation.
