mFloor - Sales Order

mFloor Selling

Information

This document describes how to work with the Sales order module and perform the following tasks:

  • Creating and adding items to a new sales order
  • Opening a previously suspended sales order document
  • Closing a sales order document to issue to the customer
  • Using the main sales order application menu

Creating and adding items to a new sales order document

To create and add items to a sales order the items must be associated to a vendor through the cost table, which must be done in SMS. If the items to add to the sales order document are already associated with a vendor, proceed below to creating a new sales order document.

To create a new sales order document for a customer follow the steps below:

  1. The sales order menu opens on the Open sales order screen. If any open sales order documents already exist they are displayed in upper section of the screen (A).
  2. To add a new sales order tap or click (1) in the upper right had corner.
  3. The new sales order screen appears displaying a list of customers(B). To scroll through the list of customers swipe the screen or if using a mouse, click and drag on the list.
  4. Tap or click a customer name to choose it.
  5. Once a customer is chosen the sales order document header appears displaying the customer name, sales order number, and associated dates.
  6. To add items to the new shipping:
    1. Type the item code in the Enter item code box at the top of the screen, or

    2. Tap or click and scan the item code, or

    3. Tap or click and a list of items for the sales order appears, choose an item by tapping or clicking it. In the case of a long list, swipe or click and drag to scroll the list.
    4. At the item screen enter the quantity to sell in the Unit box and tap or click <ENTER>
    5. Scan the next item or enter the code in Enter item code or click to go back and choose another item from Quick search.
    6. Repeat all of the above steps (a-e) to add items to the sales order.

Opening a previously suspended sales order document

When working with sales order documents users can suspend documents using the application menu or documents are automatically suspended when users tap or click when leaving a sales order.

To open a previously suspended sales order document follow the steps below:

  1. If not already in the mFloor application, open the mFloor application and log in.
  2. At the main menu of the mFloor application tap or click Selling module.
  3. At the Selling module main menu click sales order.
  4. The Open sales order menu appears showing open/suspended sales orders; if the list is long, swipe or click and scroll to view all orders.
  5. Tap or click on the desired sales order to open it.

Closing a sales order document to issue to the customer

Closing a sales order document removes it from the list of open documents seen in the Open sales order screen in mFloor.

To close a sales order document do the following steps:

  1. If not already done, select the sales order to be closed from the list of open sales order as explained above.
  2. Once the sales order is open, to close the sales order tap or click the application menu and tap or click Close document.
  3. mFloor returns to the the sales order menu.

Using the sales order application menu

When a user is in a shipping, an icon appears for the sales order application menu in the top right hand corner of the screen.

To perform the actions described below on a sales order tap or click on and then tap or click the associated menu action.

  • To show items in the current sales order tap or click List All items.
  • To show the sales order header tap or click Document Header.
  • To print the sales order tap or click Print Report.
  • To cancel the sales order tap or click Cancel Document.
  • To suspend the sales order tap or click Suspend document.
  • To close and issue sales order to customer tap or click Close Document.
