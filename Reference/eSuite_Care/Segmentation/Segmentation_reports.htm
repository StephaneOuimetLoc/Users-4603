eSuite Care - Segmentation Reports

eSuite Care > Segmentation

Information

The Segmentation reports provide information that aims at marketing strategies such as promotions and targeted offers. The information is organized into segments that group individuals with similar spending habits.

The Segmentation reports are available from eSuite Care:

  1. In the launcher, click on Segmentation.
  2. Select the right type of report from the Start window.
 

Segmentation reports:

  • By sales
  • By profit
  • By basket size
  • By visit

Segmentation by sales

The Segmentation by sales wizard offers criteria selection that includes period, date range, target and tile count (percentile rank). It allows breaking down the report information by date, store and customer.

The Segmentation by sales report provides information on the performance difference between equal groups of customers, based on sales. Data is displayed in segments.

In the bottom menu:

  • Analyze breaks down the report information by date, store and customer. It gives the choice of displaying segments by store and by date.
    It provides access to queries for further drill-down to previous results in customer lists.

Below is a brief description of the information displayed by default in the Segmentation by sales report.

COLUMN DESCRIPTION
Segment The group of individuals with similar spending habits, segmented by sales.
Count The number of customers in the segment.
Sales
Amount The amount of the total sales in the segment, from the biggest value to the smallest.
Ratio The percentage that the sales amount in the segment represents compared to the total sales amount in the report.
Transaction
Count The total number of transactions in the segment.
Ratio The percentage that the transaction count in the segment represents compared to the total transaction count in the report.
Quantity
Count The quantity of the items sold in the segment.
Ratio The percentage that the quantity of items sold in the segment represents compared to the total of the quantity of items sold in the report.
Average
Weekly sales The average amount of weekly sales per customer.
Weekly transactions The average number of weekly transactions.
Transaction The average amount of sales per transaction.
Quantity The number of items sold per transaction.
Price The average price per item.

When double-clicked, segments give access to the Sales by customer report that shows the sales by customer for the selected segment.

NOTE: This report requires additional configurations to function. See your reseller for details.

Segmentation by profit

The Segmentation by profit wizard offers criteria selection that includes period, date range, target and tile count (percentile rank). It allows breaking down the report information by date, store and customer.

The Segmentation by profit report provides sales information displayed in segments, based on the profit, to determine the most profitable customer.

In the bottom menu:

  • Analyze breaks down the report information by date, bystore and customer. It gives the choice of displaying segments by store and by date.
    It provides access to queries for further drill-down to previous results in customer lists.

Below is a brief description of the information displayed by column in the default view of the Segmentation by profit report.

COLUMN DESCRIPTION
Segment The group of individuals with similar spending habits, segmented by profit.
Count The number of customers in the segment.
Sales
Total The total amount of the sales for the segment, from biggest to smallest.
Customer The total amount of the sales for clients with a customer record.
Profit
Total The amount of the total profit for the segment.
Customer The amount of the profit for clients with a customer record.
No customer The amount of the profit for clients with no customer record.
Transaction count
Total The total number of transactions in the segment.
Customer The total number of transactions for clients with customer record.
No customer The total number of transactions for clients with no customer record.

When double-clicked, segments give access to the Profit by customer report that shows the profit by customer for the selected segment.

NOTE: This report requires additional configurations to function. See your reseller for details.
